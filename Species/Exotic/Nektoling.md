# Nektoling
The title of Nektoling is given not to a generalized lineage per se, but to an extremely unique and aberrant specimen of aquatic animal, warped physically and mentally by an abnormal event. This event could relate to an alien influence, arcane experimentation, genetic alteration, astral exposure; or many other scenarios. Regardless of the backstory for this deviation, it not only dramatically enhances the Nektoling's natural intellect, but confers their body with latent psionic powers; rendering them an unlikely proponent of artifice.

**Nektoling Traits**

**Creature Type:** Beast  
**Size:** Small or Tiny (choose the size when you select this race)  
**Speed:** 0 ft.  
**Languages.** You can read and understand Common and Aquan.

**Aquatic Being.** As a creature native to the oceans, you possess the following attributes:
- You can only breathe in water.
- You have a Swim Speed of 30 feet. While you are submerged in water, you can take the Disengage action as a Bonus Action.
- You have Darkvision out to a range of 90 feet.
- You are unable to physically hold, wear, or carry anything.
- You are immune to the Frigid Water weather condition.
- Whenever you finish a Long Rest while well-fed, roll a d4. From that point forth, you can go for an amount of weeks equal to the number rolled without needing to eat.

***Psionic Nature.*** You have Telepathy with a range of 60 feet, which you can use to communicate in the languages you know. When you do so, a creature spoken to may respond, even if they don’t see you. Additionally, you know the *Mage Hand* cantrip, and are able to cast it without spell components while making the spectral hand invisible. Constitution is your spellcasting ability for the spell.

***Unconventional Tinkerer.*** You can use Mage Hand to operate any set of tools within 5 feet of yourself (such as Alchemist's Supplies, Thieves' Tools, a Poisoner's Kit, or a Dice set; for example). You must continually use your action to cast Mage Hand for as long as your usage of the toolset lasts.

***Mechagill Suit.*** Over the course of a Long Rest, you can use Tinker's Tools and expend 40 GP worth of materials to build an inventive mechanical suit, specifically designed for beings of your kind. The suit is humanoid in form, with a metallic torso and limbs connected to a liquid-filled glass chamber at the top. The chamber has a special hatch that can be opened or closed by you with an Object Interaction. You can take the Utilize action to enter (or exit) the Mechagill Suit while the hatch is open, gaining the following attributes while you remain inside of it:
- You are considered to be underwater for the purpose of breathing.
- You can pilot the suit as if it was your own body; being able to hold, wield, manipulate, carry, and wear items as a humanoid creature would. This also allows you to write text in the languages you know.
- Your size is Medium, and you count as one size larger when determining your carry capacity.
- Your Speed becomes 30 feet, and you have a Climb Speed equal to your Speed; but are unable to benefit from your innate Swim Speed.
- You are considered a Construct for any prerequisite or effect that requires you to be a Construct.
- You are immune to the Extreme Cold weather condition, and to any spells or effects that specifically target beasts.
- You have Resistance to Psychic damage, but disadvantage on saving throws against spells and effects that deal Lightning damage.
- You lose the ability to innately cast Mage Hand; as your psionic energy is required to power your mechanical body.

***Jet Stream (Optional).*** You can innately cast the *Water Bolt* cantrip, using Constitution as your spellcasting ability for it. You can use this trait a number of times equal to your Constitution modifier or proficiency bonus (whichever is higher), regaining expended uses when you finish a Long Rest. If you are swimming in water, you become able to cast Water Bolt at will.

***Lightning Overload (Optional).*** Whenever you take 20 or more Lightning damage in a single turn while inside the Mechagill Suit, you must succeed on a DC 15 Constitution saving throw or have the Stunned condition until the end of your next turn.

# Nektoling Feats  
The following species feats are meant to expand the possibilities available to you when playing a Nektoling character.

## Reinforced Suit
*Prerequisite: Nektoling, Level 4+*

While you are inside the Mechagill Suit, your Constitution, Dexterity, or Strength score increases by 1 (your choice each time you finish a long rest). In addition, you can use Tinker's Tools to cast either *Longstrider* or *Jump* on yourself as a ritual.

## Psionic Battery
*Prerequisite: Nektoling, Level 4+*

You are granted the following benefits:
- You no longer lose the ability to innately cast Mage Hand while inside the Mechagill Suit.
- You can finish a Long Rest in 6 hours while performing light activity.
- While you are benefiting from the Unconventional Tinkerer trait, you can simultaneously operate and utilize three sets of Artisan's Tools in your possession; with the usage of each toolset being tied to a different activity.

**Example:** You could use Tinker's Tools for crafting an item, Alchemist's Supplies for brewing a potion, and Cartographer's Tools for charting a map; all at the same time. Benefiting from this usage of the Mage Hand spell requires you to maintain Concentration for the duration.
