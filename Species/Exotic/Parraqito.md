# Parraqito
These colorful, anthropomorphic parakeet people populate most warm coastal regions, where lush forests grow and where the call of the sea roars with the waves.  
Parraqitos’ wings are attached to the lower parts of their arms, in such a way that they can be uncomfortable to use during flight. However, thanks to their dexterous feet they can manipulate items in flight as well as using hands. Their multi-hued feathers shine in the sun, whether they are plundering as corsairs or striding nobly as high officials.  
**A Life at the Sea**  
For parraqitos the need to live their life at sea, or at least near it, is almost a physical necessity. Due to this fact, they are deeply involved with any nautical enterprise you could think of: from piracy and corsairing, selling and buying wares, to exploring and managing island colonies.
**Parroting & Plundering**  
They are a boastful and loud folk, who love music, sea shanties, booze, and all manner of partying. They always offer their advice or opinion, more often than not with a pinch of sarcasm or veiled taunt. They are great with words and can switch from a rough, pirate, talk to highly refined speech.

**Parraqito Traits**

**Creature Type:** Humanoid  
**Size:** Small (about 2-4 feet tall)  
**Speed:** 30 feet

As a Parraqito, you gain the following Special Traits:

***Flight.*** You have a Fly Speed equal to your Speed thanks to your winged arms. You can’t use this Fly Speed if you’re wearing Medium or Heavy armor.

***Grasping Talons.*** When flying, you can use your dexterous feet to hold items and wield weapons, instead of using your hands.  
While airborne, your talons can manipulate an object, open or close a door or container, pick up or set down a Tiny object, wield, or reload, a weapon that has the Light property, or a shield.

***Cackling Squawk.*** Once during your turn, you can use an Action to project your voice and distract creatures who hear your call.  
You manifest a 15 feet Sphere within 60 feet. Creatures you choose inside the Sphere must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw (DC is 8 + your proficiency + your chosen modifier between Intelligence, Wisdom or Charisma) or take Psychic damage equal 1d6 + your Proficiency Bonus and have Disadvantage on the next D20 Test they make before the start of your next turn.  
You can use this ability a number of times equal to your Proficiency Bonus, and you regain all uses when you finish a Long Rest.

***Parrot Speech.*** You have proficiency in the Performance skill or a different skill of your choice if you already have it.  
By listening at least for a minute to a conversation in a language you don't know, you can gather enough vocal cues to speak that language, but not read or write it. This effect remains in place until you use this feature again, at which point the newly parroted language replaces the old one.
