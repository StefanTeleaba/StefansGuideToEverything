# Path of the Megalodon
*Channel the Hunger of the Ancient Deep*  
Barbarians who follow the Path of the Megalodon become relentless hunters, driven by the scent of blood and the promise of the kill. Once their prey begins to falter, escape becomes all but impossible.

## Level 3: Predator of the Deep
You gain a Swim Speed equal to your Speed, and you can breathe underwater. While your Rage is active, your Swim Speed equals twice your Speed, and you don’t have to spend extra movement to move a creature Grappled by you if the creature is your size or smaller.

## Level 3: Ravenous Maw
While your Rage is active, you can make Unarmed Strikes with your selachian jaws. When you do, the strike deals Piercing damage equal to 1d8 plus your Strength modifier instead of the normal damage of your Unarmed Strike. In addition, when you activate your Rage and as a Bonus Action while your Rage is active, you can make one Unarmed Strike with your jaws.  
Once per turn when you hit a creature with an Unarmed Strike using your jaws, you can use one of the following options.  
***Feed.*** You gain Temporary Hit Points equal to your Rage Damage bonus.  
***Clamp Down.*** You can use both the Damage and the Grapple options of that Unarmed Strike. While a creature is Grappled by your jaws, you can’t use your jaws to make an Unarmed Strike. At the start of each of your turns, a creature Grappled by your jaws takes 1d8 Piercing damage. If a creature takes this damage, you can use Feed.

## Level 6: Blood in the Water
While your Rage is active, you gain the following benefits.  
***Scent of Blood.*** You have Blindsight with a range of 30 feet, but only for creatures that have the Bloodied condition. This range increases to 60 feet if both you and the creature are in the same body of water. Additionally, you have Advantage on any Wisdom (Perception or Survival) check you make to find a creature that is missing any of its Hit Points.  
***Bloodthirsty.*** When a creature you can see within 30 feet of you becomes Bloodied, you can take a Reaction to move up to your Speed to an unoccupied space within 5 feet of that creature. You can then make one Unarmed Strike with your jaws against it as part of the same Reaction. The range of this feature increases to 60 feet if both you and the creature are in the same body of water.

## Level 10: Strong Jaw
Your jaws become unstoppable, crushing flesh and bone with the terrible certainty of a hunting leviathan. You gain the following benefits.  
***Empowered Feed.*** When you use Feed, the Temporary Hit Points you gain equal twice your Rage Damage bonus.  
***Greater Maw.*** The damage die of your jaws increases to 2d8. Additionally, at the start of each of your turns, a creature that is Grappled by your jaws takes 2d8 Piercing damage instead of 1d8.  
***Vice Grip.*** Bloodied creatures have Disadvantage on ability checks and saving throws to avoid or escape being Grappled by you.

## Level 14: Shadow of the Colossus
When you activate your Rage, you can conjure the spectral presence of a colossal megalodon that circles you in a 10-foot Emanation. This Emanation lasts for the duration of that Rage. Once you use this feature, you can't use it again until you finish a Long Rest unless you expend a use of your Rage (no action required) to restore your use of it. The Emanation has the following properties.  
***Ancient Hunger.*** When you conjure this Emanation, you can designate creatures to be unaffected by it. Whenever the Emanation enters a creature’s space, whenever a creature enters the Emanation, and whenever a creature ends its turn there, that creature must make a Dexterity saving throw (DC 8 plus your Strength modifier and Proficiency Bonus), taking 3d6 Piercing damage on a failed save or half as much damage on a successful one. A creature makes this save only once per turn.  
***Bleeding Wound.*** On a failed save, the creature also has a Bleeding Wound until the end of your next turn. While a creature has a Bleeding Wound, it gains the Bloodied condition, regardless of its current Hit Points, and can’t regain Hit Points.
