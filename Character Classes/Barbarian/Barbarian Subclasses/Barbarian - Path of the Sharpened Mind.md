# Path of the Sharpened Mind
[*Created by CamunonZ*](https://bio.site/spaghetti0)

While no less angry than their traditional counterparts, a barbarian of this path connects with their wrath in a different way, letting it fuel their intellect instead of obfuscating it.
Rather than wild brutes, they are a breed of cunning, calculative warriors; with a mental strength and force of will that come second to none.

## Level 3: Silent Wrath
At 3rd level, rather than an explosive cannon, you learn to turn your ire into a sharpened blade.  
You are able to benefit from the Reckless Attack feature, and to apply your Rage Damage bonus on a hit, when making a melee attack roll using Dexterity.  
In addition, whenever you enter a Rage, you can choose to add your proficiency bonus to your AC for the duration.  
If you do so, your Rage requires concentration to be maintained, instead of what would be the standard requirements.

## Level 3: Focus Surge
Starting at 3rd level, as a Bonus Action, you can steel your mind to complete the task in front of you.  
Your Rage Damage bonus is added twice to the next ability check you make until the end of your next turn.  
You can use this feature a number of times equal to your proficiency bonus, regaining expended uses when you finish a short or long rest.  
Starting from 6th level, whenever you fail an ability check or saving throw, you can expend one use of this feature as a reaction to add your Rage Damage bonus to the result; potentially turning the failure into a success.

## Level 6: Scour Weakness
Starting at 6th level, you are able to direct all of your wrath onto a single target, searching intently for a crack in their defenses.  
While you are concentrating on your Rage, you can use your Bonus Action to make an Intelligence (Investigation) check against the Passive Deception score of a creature within 15 feet of you.  
On a success, the lowest number you must roll on the d20 to score a critical hit against the targeted creature decreases by 1; up to a minimum threshold of 17. This benefit lasts until your Rage ends.

## Level 10: Heightened Flow
Starting at 10th level, your brainpower spikes along with your adrenaline.  
Whenever you enter a Rage, you gain 3 benefits of your choice from the options below.
- You have resistance to psychic damage.
- You have advantage on saving throws to resist being Charmed.
- You have advantage on saving throws to resist being Frightened.
- Your attacks cannot be made at disadvantage.
- When taking the Attack action on your turn, you can replace one of your attacks with the Dodge or Disengage action.

The chosen benefits last until your Rage ends. As a Bonus Action on your turn, you can expend a hit die to replace one of your current benefits with another one from this list.

## Level 14: Ego Fortress
At 14th level, the temple of your mind cannot be sieged.  
You have permanent advantage on Constitution saving throws, which cannot be negated or canceled out in any way.  
Additionally, you can now pick from the following options while benefitting from Heightened Flow:
- You have immunity to psychic damage.
- You cannot be Charmed, Frightened, or Possessed.
- You can replace two of your other current benefits with a single Bonus Action.
