# Path of War
[*Created by the_bluez*](https://bio.site/spaghetti0)

*Kill and loot!*  
Instead of being a traditionally more tribal culture, or deriving your strength from esoteric or emotional sources, you come from a more ordered path of life, though still violent. You are a trained soldier, one that becomes a killing machine onto the battlefield, and that pushes others to follow their gruesome example.

## Level 3: Warmonger
Whenever you enter the battlefield, you inflame others to give in to their bloodlust.  
***Warcry.*** As part of the Bonus Action to initiate your Rage, you can select an ally within 60 feet. If willing, the ally can move up to half its speed or make a melee weapon Attack or an Unarmed Strike, if possible. You can target yourself. Once used, you can’t use this feature again until your current Rage ends.

## Level 6: Battle Banner
As the onslaught goes on, your own companions find strength in your fury.  
As a Bonus Action, while your Rage is active, you can share your Rage Damage bonus and give advantage on Strength checks and Strength saving throws with creatures of your choice within 30 feet, until the start of your next turn.

## Level 10: Battle Icon
Those who fall find themselves lifted up by your incredible might.  
When you strike an enemy with a melee weapon Attack or an Unarmed Strike while your Rage is active, creatures of your choice who see you can take a Reaction and roll again with advantage a saving throw to end a Condition currently affecting them. Each ally may benefit from this effect only once per Rage.

## Level 14: Warlord
Your mere presence brings on the tide of war.  
***Tide of War.*** When using Warcry, you can target up to two creatures. The creatures can immediately move up to their speed and make a melee weapon Attack or an Unarmed Strike against a target you select within their reach. You can also target unwilling creatures with this feature, possibly forcing enemies to attack each other. You can do so once and recover all expended uses at the end of a Long Rest. You can also recover one expended use by consuming a Rage use (no action required).
