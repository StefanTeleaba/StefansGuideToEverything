# Domain of the Drowned
*Fickle like a wave. Deep as the abyss.*  
Once a sailor, a pirate, perhaps a captain, or a simple passenger. The sea took you, purified you in its cold womb, and from its salt and waves you were reborn. Now you dispense its gifts and its wrath to anyone you meet.

## Level 3: Briny Curse
As a Bonus Action, you can curse a creature you can see within 60 feet. The creature must succeed on a Constitution saving throw (DC equal to your spell DC) or take 1d6 Acid damage and again 1d6 at the start of each of its turns.  
This effect lasts for 1 minute or until the target is reduced to 0 HP. As part of the Bonus Action, you can expend one spell slot to inflict a number of d6s Acid damage equal to 1 + the spell level on the first round.  
You can use this feature a number of times equal to your Wisdom modifier (minimum 1).  
You regain one expended use at the end of a Short Rest and all expended uses at the end of a Long Rest.

## Level 3: Gift of the Sea
The ocean has given you life, and as long as you serve it, it will never take it away. You gain a Swim Speed of 30 feet. If you already have a Swim Speed, increase it by 10 feet. You gain Advantage when making saving throws to resist Exhaustion due to suffocation.

## Level 3: Rescue & Sink
You can use Channel Divinity to lift your allies or crush your enemies. As a Magic action, you can present your holy symbol and choose one of the following effects.  
***Rescue.*** Choose a number of creatures you can see within 60 feet equal to your Wisdom modifier (minimum 1). You and each target gain a Swim Speed equal to their Speed (unless it would already be higher) and the ability to breathe water. This effect lasts for 1 hour.  
***Sink.*** Select a creature within 60 feet that you can see. The creature must make a Constitution saving throw (DC equal to your spell DC). If it succeeds nothing happens. If it fails it begins suffocating, gaining one Exhaustion level and it can't speak or use Vocal components for 1 minute. At the end of each of its turns, the creature can attempt the saving throw again to end the effect. If it fails again, its Exhaustion level increases by 1. If it succeeds, the creature is immune to further uses of Sink until 24 hours have passed. The creature loses all Exhaustion levels if the effect ends.

## Level 6: Drowned’s Grasp
Once per turn, when a creature within 60 feet of you takes damage from one of your Cleric spells, you can take a Reaction to give them the Prone condition. If the target is submerged in water, it is also pushed down 10 feet.  
You can use this feature a number of times equal to your Wisdom modifier, and regain all uses when you finish a Short or Long Rest.

## Level 17: Drowned Prophet
You get the following benefits.  
***Blessing of the Deep.*** You can breathe underwater, your Swim Speed increases by 10feet, and you can choose to walk normally on the seabed.  
***Call of the Abyss.*** As a Magic Action, you can summon the Drowner, a fearsome avatar of the Drowned. Maintaining the summoning requires Concentration.  
The Drowner appears in a location you can see within 120 feet and remains for 1 minute or until brought to 0 HP. You can use this feature once, and you regain all expended uses when you finish a long rest. In combat, the Drowner acts during your turn. It can move and use its reaction on its own, but its only action is Dodge unless you use a Bonus Action to command it to perform one of its actions in the stat block, or other actions. If you lose Concentration the Drowner immediately disappears.
