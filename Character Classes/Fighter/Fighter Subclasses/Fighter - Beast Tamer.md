# Beast Tamer

*Fight Alongside a Bestial Spirit*  
A Beast Tamer forges powerful connections with beasts and nature spirits, inspiring such ethereal beings to manifest and journey on far-flung adventures.

## Level 3: Primal Companion
You magically summon a primal spirit that adopts the form of a beast and accompanies you on your adventures. Choose its stat block: *Primal Guardian* or *Primal Striker*. In addition, choose an environment that will modify the creature's stat block: Land, Sea, or Sky. You also determine the kind of animal it is, choosing a kind appropriate for the stat block. Whatever companion you choose, it bears eldritch markings indicating its otherworldly origin.  
The companion is Friendly to you and your allies and obeys your commands. It vanishes if you die.  
***The Beast in Combat.*** In combat, the companion acts during your turn. It can move and use its Reaction on its own, but the only action it takes is the Dodge action unless you take a Bonus Action to command it to take an action in its stat block or some other action. You can also sacrifice one of your attacks when you take the Attack action to command the beast to take the Beast's Strike action. If you have the Incapacitated condition, the companion acts on its own and isn't limited to the Dodge action.  
***Restoring or Replacing the Beast.*** If the companion has died within the last hour, you can take a Magic action to touch it and expend a use of your Second Wind. The companion returns to life immediately with all its Hit Points restored.  
Whenever you finish a Short or Long Rest, you can summon a different primal companion, which appears in an unoccupied space within 5 feet of you. You choose its stat block and appearance. If you already have a beast from this feature, the old one vanishes when the new one appears.

## Level 7: Shared Second Wind
When you use your Second Wind to regain Hit Points, the companion also regains Hit Points equal to 1d10 plus your Fighter level.

## Level 7: Exceptional Training
Commanding your Primal Companion on your turn no longer requires a Bonus Action, and the Primal Companion’s AC gains a +2 bonus and its Hit Points increase by a number equal to twice your Fighter level.  
In addition, whenever it hits with an attack roll and deals damage, it can deal your choice of Force damage or its normal damage type.

## Level 10: Bestial Fury
When you command your Primal Companion beast to take the Beast’s Strike action, the beast can use it twice, and the damage of each Beast Strike attack improves by +2.  
In addition, if the Primal Companion needs to make a Saving Throw, it can use your bonus instead of its own if it’s higher.

## Level 15: Shared Resilience
When your companion fails a saving throw, you can take a Reaction to expend a use of your Indomitable feature. The companion can immediately reroll the saving throw with a bonus equal to your Fighter level and must use the new roll.

## Level 15: Tandem Shift
Whenever you activate your Second Wind with a Bonus Action, your companion can take a Reaction to move up to half its Speed without provoking Opportunity Attacks.

## Level 18: Shared Action Surge
When you use your Action Surge, the companion can also take one additional action.

## Level 18: Unshakable Bravery
You and your companion have Immunity to the Charmed and Frightened conditions while you are within 60 feet of eachother.

**Primal Guardian**  
*Medium or Large Beast, Neutral*  

**AC** 13 plus your Constitution modifier  
**HP** 6 plus six times your Fighter level (the beast has a number of Hit Dice [d10s] equal to your Fighter level)  
**Speed** 30 ft., Fly 40 ft. (Sky only), Swim 40 ft. (Sea only)

|         |    | MOD | SAVE |
|---------|----|-----|------|
| **STR** | 15 | +2  | +2   |
| **DEX** | 12 | +2  | +2   |
| **CON** | 16 | +3  | +3   |
| **INT** | 4  | -3  | -3   |
| **WIS** | 12 | +1  | +1   |
| **CHA** | 6  | -2  | -2   |

**Senses** Darkvision 60 ft., Passive Perception 11  
**Languages** Understands the languages you know  
**CR** None (XP 0; PB equals your Proficiency Bonus)

**Traits**  
***Protective Instinct.*** When the guardian becomes Bloodied, it gains Temporary Hit Points equal to half of its maximum Hit Points and is enraged for 10 minutes. While enraged, Hostile creatures within 5 feet have Disadvantage on attack rolls against creatures other than the guardian.  
***Vigilant.*** The guardian can't be surprised.  
***Wild Bond.*** Add your Proficiency Bonus to any ability check, attack roll, or saving throw the beast makes.

**Actions**  
***Beast's Strike.*** *Melee Attack Roll:* Bonus equals your attack modifier using Strength or Dexterity, reach 5 ft. *Hit:* 1d4 + 2 plus your Strength or Dexterity modifier Bludgeoning, Piercing, or Slashing damage (your choice).

**Reactions**  
***Body Block.*** *Trigger:* When a creature the beast can see attacks a target other than itself that is within 5 feet of it. *Response:* The guardian takes the damage instead of the target.

**Primal Striker**  
*Medium Beast, Neutral*  

**AC** 13 plus your Constitution modifier  
**HP** 4 plus four times your Fighter level (the beast has a number of Hit Dice [d6s] equal to your Fighter level)  
**Speed** 40 ft., Fly 60 ft. (Sky only), Swim 60 ft. (Sea only)

|         |    | MOD | SAVE |
|---------|----|-----|------|
| **STR** | 15 | +2  | +2   |
| **DEX** | 15 | +2  | +2   |
| **CON** | 15 | +2  | +2   |
| **INT** | 4  | -3  | -3   |
| **WIS** | 12 | +1  | +1   |
| **CHA** | 6  | -2  | -2   |

**Senses** Darkvision 60 ft., Passive Perception 11  
**Languages** Understands the languages you know  
**CR** None (XP 0; PB equals your Proficiency Bonus)

**Traits**  
***Enraged Strikes.*** The beast's attack rolls have Advantage against any enemy that is within 5 feet of you.  
***Wild Bond.*** Add your Proficiency Bonus to any ability check, attack roll, or saving throw the beast makes.  
***Aquatic Assault (Sea Only).*** While the beast is submerged in water, it has Advantage on attack rolls against creatures without a Swim Speed submerged in water.  
***Flyby (Sky Only).*** The beast doesn't provoke Opportunity Attacks when it flies out of an enemy's reach.

**Actions**  
***Beast's Strike.*** *Melee Attack Roll:* Bonus equals your attack modifier using Strength or Dexterity, reach 5 ft. *Hit:* 1d8 + 2 plus your Strength or Dexterity modifier Bludgeoning, Piercing, or Slashing damage (your choice).

**Reactions**  
***Harry (Land Only).*** *Trigger:* When a creature the beast can see within 5 feet of it makes an attack roll. *Response:* The triggering attack roll has Advantage.
