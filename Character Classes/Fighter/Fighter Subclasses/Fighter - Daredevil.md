# Daredevil
*Critical Role, Campaign Four*

## Level 3: 

***Daring Strike.*** Once per turn when you take the Attack action, you can attempt a daring strike. On a Hit, you can expend a use of your Second Wind to deal extra damage. Rather than regaining Hit Points, you roll 1d10 and add the number rolled to the damage roll. On a Miss, the next attack roll against you before the start of your next turn has Advantage.

***Ride the Threshold (formerly Ride the Edge).*** Whenever you activate your Second Wind with a Bonus Action while Bloodied, rather than regaining Hit Points, you instead gain Temporary Hit Points equal to 1d10 plus your Fighter level. You can also expend one of your Hit Point Dice instead of expending one of your Second Wind uses.
