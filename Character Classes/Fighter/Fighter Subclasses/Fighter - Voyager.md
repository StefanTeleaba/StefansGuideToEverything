# Voyager
[*Created by PerfectlyCircularSeal*](https://bio.site/spaghetti0)

*Teamwork Makes The Dream Work*  
Your treasure is actually the friends you made along the way. Meeting new people and learning about their ideas, morals and techniques is what drives you to seek out adventure. Harnessing this experience for yourself and others is what you do best, elevating everyone’s capabilities.

## Level 3: Avidity
You aim to learn from every place you visit.  
***Cultured.*** You learn one language of your choice.  
***Curious.*** Choose two skills, two artisan’s tools, or one of each. You have proficiency with the two chosen options.

## Level 3: Encouragement
As a Bonus Action, you can encourage yourself or an ally that can hear you within 30 feet of yourself to overcome the hardship they face. The target gains a number of Temporary Hit Points equal to twice your Fighter level, which last for 1 minute.  
You can use this feature four times and regain all expended uses when you finish a Long Rest.  
Once within the duration, if you start your turn within 5 feet of the target, you can encourage the target again (no action required). It regains any of the Temporary Hit Points it lost, which last for the remainder of the duration.

## Level 3: Trusty Companion
You and any ally within a 5-foot Emanation that originates from you can reroll a weapon’s damage die if it rolled a 1 on that die.

## Level 7: Full of Spirit
You possess an infectious enthusiasm. Your Encouragement improves in the following ways.  
***Fellowship.*** When you target an ally with your Encouragement, you also gain Temporary Hit Points equal to half your Fighter level, which last for 1 minute.
***Tenacity.*** While the target of your Encouragement has any of the Temporary Hit Points left, it can reroll one attack roll or saving throw on each of its turns. It must use the new roll.

## Level 10: Diverse Training
You believe that only through diverse experience can one truly gain mastery over themselves. You gain one feat of your choice for which you qualify, short of the Ability Score Improvement feat.

## Level 15: Old Faithful
Your Trusty Companion is now a 10-foot Emanation and you and any ally within the area can treat a 1 or 2 on a weapon’s damage die as the maximum value on that die instead of rerolling the die.

## Level 18: Valor
You truly bring out the best in others. While the target of your Encouragement has any of the Temporary Hit Points left, it can’t have Disadvantage on D20 Tests to which it adds its Proficiency Bonus.
