# Oath of the Captain
*Steer Your Crew To Victory*  
Paladins that sail the rough seas of the world often swear a captain’s oath. They vow to protect both ship and crew against the dangers on and in the water. Using their inspiring magic, these paladins build unity and bravery among their crew mates.  
These paladins share the following tenets:
- Inspire and facilitate teamwork
- Be exemplary in both success and failure
- Be the first to enter and last to leave

## Level 3: Oath of the Captain Spells
The magic of your oath ensures you always have certain spells ready; when you reach a Paladin level specified in the Oath of the Captain Spells table, you thereafter always have the listed spells prepared.

**Oath of the Captain Spells**

## Level 3: Rile Up
As a Magic Action, you can expend one use of your Channel Divinity to rile up your allies with a magically imbued speech. Choose a number of creatures up to your Charisma modifier (minimum of one) within 30 feet of yourself that can hear you. Each target has Advantage on any D20 Test it makes using Strength, Dexterity or Wisdom until the start of your next turn.

## Level 3: Seaborne Spirit
Crashing waves and a salt water smell is what fuels your determination. You have a Swim Speed equal to your Speed and you also gain a bonus to your Wisdom (Survival or Perception) checks. The bonus equals your Charisma modifier (minimum of +1).

## Level 7: Aura of Comradery
When you use your Lay On Hands, you can target any ally within your Aura of Protection. In addition, if the target of your Lay On Hands has the Bloodied condition, you double the number of Hit Points you restore to it for that use.

## Level 15: All Hands On Deck
You can take a Bonus Action to call upon your crew. Each ally within 60 feet that can hear you can immediately move up to their Speed without provoking Opportunity Attacks.  
You can use this feature a number of times equal to your Charisma modifier (minimum of once), and you regain all expended uses when you finish a Long Rest.

## Level 20: King of the Hull
As a Bonus Action, you can empower your Aura of Protection, granting the benefits below for 1 minute or until you end them (no action required). Once you use this feature, you can't use it again until you finish a Long Rest. You can also restore your use of it by expending a level 5 spell slot (no action required).  
***Heave Ho.*** Your Aura of Protection is now a 90-foot Emanation.  
***Shipshape.*** You and your allies within the aura have Immunity to the Grappled, Restrained and Stunned conditions. If a Grappled, Restrained or Stunned ally enters the aura, that condition has no effect on that ally while there.
