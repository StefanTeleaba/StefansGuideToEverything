# Stowaway
*“The most dangerous passenger is the one you didn't know you had.”*  
Whether it’s the hold of a ship, shadow of a giant, or the folds of a noble’s cloak, the Stowaway finds safety in the spaces others ignore.

## Level 3: Under Foot
You can move through the space of any creature that is your size or larger, and you can end your move in such a creature’s space without penalty. While you share a space with another creature, you gain a benefit based on your relationship to them:  
***Ally:*** You have Half Cover.  
***Enemy:*** Once per round, you have Advantage on attack rolls against that creature.  
A creature whose space you occupy does not treat you as difficult terrain.

## Level 3: Tight Squeeze
You can move like normal when moving through a space sized for a creature one size smaller than you and have advantage when hiding inside of objects such as chests or barrels.

## Level 9: Barnacle Tactics
When you end your move in the space of a creature larger than you, you can use a bonus action to try and Cling to them. Make an Athletics or Acrobatics check contested by their own. On a success, you are clinging. While clinging, you move with the creature and do not provoke opportunity attacks.  
When the creature you are clinging to is attacked by another source, you can use your Reaction to either make one melee weapon attack against that creature or grant the attacker Advantage on their roll.  
A creature you are clinging to or within 5 feet of you, can use its action to attempt to dislodge you by making an Athletics or Acrobatics check contested by your own. On a success, you are no longer clinging. The cling also ends if you move out of the space, let go (as a reaction to the creature moving on their turn or with no action required on your turn), are incapacitated, or are knocked prone.

## Level 13: Hitchhiker
If a creature you are clinging to attempts to teleport or travel to another plane, you can choose to travel with them.  
Additionally, you have advantage on ability checks and saving throws against effects that would attempt to move you, including those used to attempt to end your clinging.

## Level 17: Master Stowaway
You have become a master of using others as cover. While you are sharing a space with a creature (ally or enemy), you gain the benefits of Three-Quarters Cover against all attacks and effects originating from outside that space.
