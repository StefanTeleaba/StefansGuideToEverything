# Hellbinder
[*Created by PerfectlyCircularSeal*](https://bio.site/spaghetti0)

*You Made A Deal A Devil Couldn’t Refuse*  
You have learned the art of the devilish deals. Through infernal means, you have recruited a true devil as your right hand in all manner of antics. Channeling the power of the Nine Hells, you use any means necessary to get what you want.

## Level 3: Devil’s Words
You have learned the arts of conversation from the infernal ranks. You know Infernal or one language of your choice if you already know it.

## Level 3: Infernal Contract
You summon a Devilish Subordinate to your service. It uses the Devilish Subordinate stat block, though you determine its appearance. The devil is Friendly to you and your allies and obeys your commands. It vanishes if you die.  
***The Devil in Combat.*** In combat, the devil acts during your turn. It can move and use its Reaction on its own, but the only action it takes is the Dodge action. As a Bonus Action, or as part of the Bonus Action you use to activate your Cunning Action, you can command the devil to take an action and/or Bonus Action from its stat block, or some other action. If you have the Incapacitated condition, the devil acts on its own and isn’t limited to the Dodge action.  
If you command the devil to make an attack roll that meets the requirements to use your Sneak Attack, you can use it on its attack rather than one of your own that turn.  
***Restoring or Replacing the Devil.*** If the devil dies, you can perform a 1-minute ritual to magically summon it again. At the end of the ritual the devil appears in an unoccupied space within 5 feet of you.

## Level 3: Indenture of Agony
As a Magic action, you can enact an infernal indenture to a creature that you can see within 5 feet of yourself or your Devilish Subordinate, commanding it to carry out some service or refrain from an action or a course of activity as you decide. The target must succeed on a Charisma saving throw (DC 8 plus your Charisma modifier and Proficiency Bonus) or have the Charmed condition for 1 hour. The target automatically succeeds if it can't understand your command.  
While Charmed, the creature can add a d4 to the result of any D20 Test it makes to achieve the command. In addition, if the creature acts in a manner directly counter to your command, it takes Psychic damage. To determine the amount of damage, you roll a number of d4s equal to your Charisma modifier (minimum 1d4). The creature can only take this damage once every minute and immediately after the creature takes the damage it can repeat the initial saving throw, ending the effect on a successful save.  
You can issue any command you choose, except any activity that would result in certain death. The effect ends early if you issue a suicidal command or if you gain the Incapacitated condition.  
Once you use this feature, you can’t use it again until you finish a Short or Long Rest.

## Level 9: Contingency Plan
Your infernal deeds always come well prepared. If you fail a Deception check, you have Advantage on the next Intimidation or Persuasion check you make within the next minute.

## Level 9: Esquire
You have refined your devilish ways and taught your underling everything you know. You gain the following benefits.  
***Infernal Protege.*** Your Devilish Subordinate gains the benefits of your Evasion and Uncanny Dodge.  
***Refined Scripture.*** The die size of the bonus and damage for your Indenture of Agony increases to a d6.

## Level 13: Hellish Ways
You have fully embraced the ways of the Nine Hells. You gain the following benefits.  
***Devil’s Guile.*** You and your Devilish Subordinate have Immunity to the Charmed and Frightened condition.  
***Grand Entrance.*** When a creature within 60 feet of you gains the Charmed or Frightened condition, you can take a Reaction to teleport yourself or your Devilish Subordinate to an unoccupied space within 5 feet of it.

## Level 17: Fine Print
You place your infernal clauses on whoever you hurt. You gain the following Cunning Strike option.  
***Obligate (Cost: 5d6).*** The target must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw or be forced to adhere to your infernal fine print. On its next turn, you mentally command its movement and actions. Creatures that have Immunity to the Charmed condition have Advantage on their saving throw.

### Devilish Subordinate
*Small Fiend (Devil), Lawful Evil*  

**AC** 13 plus your Charisma modifier  
**HP** 5 plus five times your Rogue level (the devil has a number of Hit Dice [d6s] equal to your Rogue level)  
**Speed** 30 ft.

|         |    | MOD | SAVE |
|---------|----|-----|------|
| **STR** | 6  | -2  | -2   |
| **DEX** | 16 | +3  | +3   |
| **CON** | 13 | +1  | +1   |
| **INT** | 11 | +0  | +0   |
| **WIS** | 12 | +1  | +1   |
| **CHA** | 17 | +3  | +3   |

**Resistances** Cold  
**Immunities** Fire, Poison, Poisoned  
**Senses** Darkvision 120 ft. (unimpeded by magical Darkness), Passive Perception 11  
**Languages** Common, Infernal  
**CR** None (XP 0; PB equals your Proficiency Bonus)

#### Traits
**Devious Duo.** While you are within 15 feet of your Devilish Subordinate you can add 1d4 to the result of any ability check you make using Charisma.  
**Tutelage.** The devil shares all of your skill and saving throw profi ciencies.

#### Actions
**Brand.** *Melee Attack Roll:* PB plus your Charisma modifier, reach 5 ft. *Hit:* 1d6 + 3 + your Charisma modifier Fire, Piercing or Poison damage (your choice each time it makes the attack).  
**Shape-Shift.** The devil shape-shifts to resemble a Small or Medium Humanoid (your choice of species), or it returns to its true form. Its statistics are the same in each form.

#### Bonus Actions
**Infernal Toolset.** The devil teleports one item of your choice that you are holding or have on your person into its hands, provided it is on the same plane of existence as you. After 10 minutes, the item reappears in an unoccupied space within 5 feet of you.  
**Sly Action.** The devil takes the Dash, Disengage, or Hide action (your choice).
