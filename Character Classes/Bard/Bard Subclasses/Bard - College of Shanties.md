# College of Shanties
*Lift The Spirits Of Your Crew*  
On the many voyages across the seas, there are times when spirits sink lower than the orlop deck. In those dire moments, only a swinging shanty can bolster a crew’s morale. Bards who dedicate their craft to these ship-stirring songs imbue magic into every word and note.

## Level 3: Sailor’s Shanty
As a Bonus Action, you can expend one use of your Bardic Inspiration to start a magical song for up to 1 minute. The song ends early if you dismiss it (no action required) or you have the Incapacitated condition.  
Roll a number of your Bardic Inspiration Dice equal to 1 plus the number of allies within 30 feet of yourself that can hear you, up to maximum number of allies equal to your Charisma modifier (minimum of 1).  
The song bestows a number of Temporary Hit Points equal to the roll’s total plus half your Bard level, which you can divide among any creature of your choice within range. These Temporary Hit Points last until the song ends.  
While a creature has any of these Temporary Hit Points left, it gains one of the following effects (you choose each time you activate this feature).  
***Hauling.*** The song grants them power and determination. The target has Advantage on Strength checks and Strength saving throws.  
***Heaving.*** The song grants them focus and dedication. The target’s movement doesn’t trigger Opportunity Attacks.

## Level 3: Sea Legs
Your time on the great waters have hardened you, granting you the following benefits.  
***Sails And Seas.*** You have a Climb and Swim Speed equal to your Speed.  
***Scurvy Dog.*** You have Advantage on ability checks and saving throws you make to avoid gaining Exhaustion levels or to decrease its level if you have any.

## Level 6: Bound By Song
While a target of your Sailor’s Shanty has any of the Temporary Hit Points left, you can cast your Bard Spells as though you were in the space of that creature.

## Level 14: Rowdy Crowd
The magical energy emanating from you and your crew chanting shanties disrupts the minds of those hostile to you. While a target of your Sailor’s Shanty has any of the Temporary Hit Points left, each creature within 5 feet of it has Disadvantage on its saving throws against your Bard spells.
