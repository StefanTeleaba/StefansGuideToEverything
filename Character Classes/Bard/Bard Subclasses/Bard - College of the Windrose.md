# College of the Windrose
*Conduct the Wind to Roil or Fly*  
Like a conductor guiding the band through its performance, you guide the element of air through its own. Bards who learn to use their magic this way live a life of freedom, and like the winds themselves, never stay in one place too long.

## Level 3: Baton of Gales
You have created a baton that is infused with the elemental energy from the Plane of Air. It is a Tiny object, and you can use it as a Spellcasting Focus for your Bard spells.  
While holding your baton, you always have the Message and Gust of Wind spells prepared, and you can cast Gust of Wind without expending a spell slot. You can cast it this way a number of times equal to your Charisma modifier (minimum of once), and you regain all uses when you finish a Long Rest.  
As a Magic action you may also use your baton to create one of the following minor magical effects within 30 feet of yourself:  
***Blast.*** Choose one Medium or smaller creature you can see. They must succeed a Strength saving throw or be pushed up to 5 feet away from you.  
***Breeze.*** You create a harmless sensory effect using air in a 5 foot space within range, such as the sound of whistling wind or clothing to ripple in a breeze.  
***Push.*** You create a small burst of air to push any object that is not being worn or carried and weighs no more than 5 pounds. The object is pushed 10 feet in a direction of your choice and isn’t forceful enough to deal damage.  
If you lose the baton, you may perform a 1-hour ritual to magically create a replacement. This can be done as part of a Short or Long Rest, and destroys the old baton.

## Level 3: Wind Tempo
When you give a creature a Bardic Inspiration dice, their speed is doubled until the end of their next turn and they can immediately use their Reaction to move up to their speed without provoking Opportunity Attacks. Additionally, the next time they hit with a weapon attack, it deals extra Force damage equal to a roll of your Bardic Inspiration dice.

## Level 6: Allegro
When you use Wind Tempo, the creature gains a Fly speed equal to their movement speed until the end of their next turn.

## Level 6: Vivacious Roil
While holding your baton, you always have the Fly and Wind Wall spells prepared. You can cast one of them without expending a spell slot, and you regain the ability to cast them in this way when you finish a Long Rest.

## Level 14: Aria of New Horizons
Using your Baton, you can use a Magic action to cast Wind Walk without expending a spell slot. When cast this way, you may instead target a Vehicle of Gargantuan size or smaller, and any creature on-board or inside of it. If you target a Vehicle, all creatures that would also be targeted by the spell must be willing creatures. While the spell is cast, you may choose to revert the Vehicle from cloud form, and when you do, all creatures on-board or inside of it must also transform with it.  
Once you use this feature, you can’t do so again until you finish a Long Rest.
