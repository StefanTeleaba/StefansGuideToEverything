### Plate Armor, Adamantine, Masterwork
*Heavy Armor*  

**AC:** 18  
**Strength:** 15  
**Stealth:** Disadvantage  
**Weight:** 65 lb.  
**Cost:** 1,500 GP + 300 GP + 65x 100 GP = 8,300 GP  

***Adamantine.*** While you're wearing this suit of armor, any Critical Hit against you becomes a normal hit.
