# Iron Net
*Advanced equipment*  
*150 gp, 20 lb.*

This net is comprised of tiny interlocking iron rings. It acts as a normal Net, with the following changes:
- The Iron Net's range is 10 feet.
- The DC of the Strength (Athletics) check to escape is 15.
- The Iron Net has AC 13 and 15 HP.

**Source:** *Grim Hollow: Player's Guide 2024 v5.5, p.126*
