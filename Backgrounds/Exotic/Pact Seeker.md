# Pact Seeker
You had a purpose in mind. Perhaps you were seeking out knowledge or power. Or maybe you were in search of hidden secrets. One way or another, your journey lead you to seek out an extraplanar entity. Once you made contact, a deal was struck, and your life changed.

**Ability Scores:** Constitution, Intelligence, Charisma  
**Feat:** Choose one Planar Pact feat  
**Skill Proficiencies:** Arcana and Persuasion  
**Tool Proficiencies:** Calligrapher’s Supplies  
**Equipment:** Choose A or B: (A) Book, Calligrapher’s Supplies, Ink, Ink Pen, Parchment (10 sheets), Traveler’s Clothes, 2 GP; or (B) 50 GP.
