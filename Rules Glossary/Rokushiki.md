# Rokushiki
*Homebrew*

**Geppo**  
You can push off cushions of air as if on solid ground. You have a *Fly Speed* equal to your *Speed*, and can hover. If your Speed is 0, you fall.

**Tekkai**  
Your muscles harden to the level of iron. You gain a bonus to your AC equal to your Strength modifier (minimum of +1).

**Rankyaku**  
You can be fast enough to create slicing winds. When you make an attack with either a weapon or an *Unarmed Strike*, your reach increases by a number of feet equal to 5 times your Proficiency Bonus.

**Soru**  
Your leg strength is explosive enough to seemingly cause your body to disappear. You can jump up to a number of feet equal to 10 times your Proficiency Bonus by spending 10 feet of movement. By spending 30 feet of movement, you cause your body to become blurred. Creatures have Disadvantage on attack rolls against you until the start of your next turn.

**Shigan**  
A bullet isn't needed to pierce the human body. Whenever you deal damage with your *Unarmed Strike*, it can deal your choice of Piercing damage or its normal damage type.

**Kami-e**  
If a creature hits you with a melee attack, you can take a Reaction to add your Proficiency Bonus to your Armor Class, potentially causing the attack to miss you. You gain this bonus to your AC against melee attacks until the start of your next turn.

**Rokuogan**  
As an Action, you unleash a devastating shockwave. Each creature in a 60-foot Cone originating from you makes a Dexterity saving throw, taking Force equal to a number of d10s equal to your level on a failed save or half as much damage on a successful one. Once you use this feature, you can't do so again until you finish a Short or Long Rest.
