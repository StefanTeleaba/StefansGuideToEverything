# Titan
*Specialization Feat (Prerequisites: Level 8+, Strength 15+, Great Weapon Master)*

You gain the following benefits.

***Ability Score Increase.*** Increase your Strength score by 1, to a maximum of 20.

***Bone Crusher.*** When you hit a creature with a weapon that has the Heavy property, the impact staggers your target. It has Disadvantage on Constitution saving throws until the end of your next turn.

***Titanic Strike.*** When you make an attack roll with a weapon that has the Heavy property against a creature that is Large or larger, the number you need to roll on the d20 to score a Critical Hit is 1 lower than normal.
