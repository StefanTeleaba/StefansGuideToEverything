# Wrestler
*Specialization Feat (Prerequisites: Level 8+, Strength or Dexterity 15+, Grappler)*

You gain the following benefits.

***Ability Score Increase.*** Increase your Strength or Dexterity score by 1, to a maximum of 20.

***Flying Hands.*** Once per turn, you can move up to 10 feet without provoking Opportunity Attacks immediately before you make an Unarmed Strike against a creature.

***Tighten Grip.*** When you make an Unarmed Strike against a creature Grappled by you, you can forgo dealing damage and tighten your grip on it instead. If you do, it has Disadvantage on d20 Tests it makes using Strength or Dexterity until the start of your next turn.
