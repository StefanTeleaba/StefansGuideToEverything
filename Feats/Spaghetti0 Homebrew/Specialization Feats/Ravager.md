# Ravager
*Specialization Feat (Prerequisites: Level 8+, Strength or Dexterity 15+, Charger)*

You gain the following benefits.

***Ability Score Increase.*** Increase your Strength or Dexterity score by 1, to a maximum of 20.

***Full Throttle.*** You can take the Dash action as a Bonus Action.

***Reinforcement.*** Immediately after an ally moves into a space within 5 feet of a creature that you can see within 30 feet of yourself, you can take a Reaction to move up to your Speed straight towards that creature. If you end your movement within 5 feet of the target, you can make an attack against it with a Melee weapon or Unarmed Strike as part of that same Reaction. On a hit, you can apply your Charge Attack to that attack.
