# Living Fortress
*Specialization Feat (Prerequisites: Level 8+, Heavy Armor Master)*

You gain the following benefits.

***Ability Score Increase.*** Increase your Strength or Constitution score by 1, to a maximum of 20.

***Enhanced Damage Reduction.*** When you use your Damage Reduction, you can reduce the damage dealt to you by an additional amount equal to your Constitution modifier (minimum of 1). In addition, you can apply your Damage Reduction to damage of any type except Psychic.

***Heavy Weight.*** If you are moved against your will while you are wearing Heavy armor, the distance you are moved is reduced by 10 feet.
