# Trapper
*Origin Feat*  
You gain the following benefits.  
***Eye for Detail.*** You have Advantage on any Intelligence (Investigation) check you make as part of the Study action.  
***Swift Tracker.*** You don’t have Disadvantage on Wisdom (Perception or Survival) checks while traveling at a Fast pace, and you have Advantage on such checks while traveling at a Normal pace.  
***Trap Expert.*** You can take a Bonus Action, instead of a Utilize action, to set a Hunting Trap. When you set a Hunting Trap, you add your Proficiency Bonus to the DC of the saving throw to avoid the trap and the DC of the check to escape it.
