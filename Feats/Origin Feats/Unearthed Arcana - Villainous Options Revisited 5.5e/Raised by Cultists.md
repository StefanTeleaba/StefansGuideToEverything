# Raised by Cultists
*Origin Feat*  
You gain the following benefits.  
***Bloody Revelation.*** When you become Bloodied, you can take a Reaction to gain Heroic Inspiration.  
***Communal Caster.*** When an ally within 5 feet of you makes a Constitution saving throw to maintain Concentration, you can take a Reaction to give your ally Advantage on the save.
