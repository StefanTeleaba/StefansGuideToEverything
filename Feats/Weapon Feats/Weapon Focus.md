# Weapon Focus
*General Feat (Prerequisite: Level 4+)*

Choose one Simple or Martial weapon with which you have proficiency or your Unarmed Strike.
***Ability Score Increase.*** Increase your Strength or Dexterity by 1, to a maximum of 20.  
***Weapon Focus.*** You gain a +1 bonus to attack rolls and a +2 bonus to damage rolls with the selected weapon.  
***Repeatable.*** You can take this feat more than once.
