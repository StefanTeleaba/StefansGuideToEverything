# Boon of the Cleansed Heart
*Epic Boon Feat (Prerequisite: Level 19+)*  
You gain the following benefits.  
***Ability Score Increase.*** Increase one ability score of your choice by 1, to a maximum of 30.  
***Cleanse Heart.*** You can cast Dispel Evil and Good without expending a spell slot. You can’t use the spell’s Dismissal special function when you cast it in this way.  
***Radiant Reflection.*** You have Immunity to Necrotic damage. When you would be subjected to Necrotic damage and don’t have the Incapacitated condition, you can deal 2d8 Radiant damage to each creature of your choice within a 10-foot Emanation originating from yourself.
