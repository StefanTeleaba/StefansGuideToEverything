# Reinforced Mind
*Specialization Feat (Prerequisites: Level 8+, Mage Slayer)*

You gain the following benefits.  
***Ability Score Increase.*** Increase one ability score of your choice by 1, to a maximum of 20.  
***Reinforced Mind.*** You can use your *Guarded Mind* feature a number of times equal to your Proficiency Bonus. You regain one expended use when you finish a Short Rest, and you regain all expended uses when you finish a Long Rest.
