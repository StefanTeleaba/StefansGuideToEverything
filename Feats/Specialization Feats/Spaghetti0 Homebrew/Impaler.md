# Impaler
*Specialization Feat (Prerequisites: Level 8+, Piercer)*

You gain the following benefits.

***Ability Score Increase.*** Increase your Strength or Dexterity score by 1, to a maximum of 20.

***Enhanced Puncture.*** When using your Puncture, you can reroll any of the damage dice for the attack.

***Perforate.*** When you hit a creature with an attack that deals Piercing damage, you can temporarily destroy its defenses. Its Armor Class is reduced by 2 for 1 minute. At the end of each of its turns, the target can make a Constitution saving throw (DC 8 plus the ability modifier of the score increased by this feat and your Proficiency Bonus), ending the effect on a success. You can use this feature a number of times equal to your Proficiency Bonus and regain all expended uses when you finish a Long Rest.
