# Bravado
*Specialization Feat (Prerequisites: Level 8+, Dexterity 15+, Defensive Duelist)*

You gain the following benefits.

***Ability Score Increase.*** Increase your Dexterity or Charisma score by 1, to a maximum of 20.

***Mano A Mano.*** When you make an attack roll using a Finesse weapon, you can add half of your Proficiency Bonus to the attack and damage rolls, provided there are no allies within 5 feet of you or your target.

***Swipe.*** If you use your Parry and a creature misses you with a Melee attack, you can make one attack using a Finesse weapon against it (no action required). On a hit, you don’t add your ability modifier to the damage roll. Once you use this feature, you can’t use it again until you use your Parry again.
