# Winged Hussar
*Specialization Feat (Prerequisites: Level 8+, Mounted Combatant)*

You gain the following benefits.

***Ability Score Increase.*** Increase your Strength, Dexterity or Wisdom score by 1, to a maximum of 20.

***Cavalry Strike.*** While mounted, when you command your mount to take the Dash action, you can make one attack using a Melee weapon or Unarmed Strike as part of that action. The target of the attack has to be within range of your weapon and must be within reach at any point during your movement.

***Fearless Rider.*** Your Mounted Strike works against creatures that are the same size as your mount or smaller.
