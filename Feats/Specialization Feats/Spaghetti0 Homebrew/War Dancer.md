# War Dancer
*Specialization Feat (Prerequisites: Level 8+, Strength or Dexterity 15+, Dual Wielder)*

You gain the following benefits.

***Ability Score Increase.*** Increase your Strength or Dexterity score by 1, to a maximum of 20.

***Masterful Dual Wielding.*** You can treat all Melee weapons that lack the Two-Handed property as if they have the Light property.

***Swirling Steel.*** If your attack roll with a Melee weapon that lacks the Two-Handed property misses a creature, you can deal damage to that creature equal to the ability modifier you used to make the attack roll. This damage is the same type dealt by the weapon, and the damage can be increased only by increasing the ability modifier. Immediately after you deal this damage to the target, you can make an additional attack with the same weapon (no action required). This attack must be made against a different creature within the reach of your weapon. Once you make this additional attack, you can’t make it again until the start of your next turn.
