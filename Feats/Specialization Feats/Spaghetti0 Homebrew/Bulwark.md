# Bulwark
*Specialization Feat (Prerequisites: Level 8+, Shield Master)*

You gain the following benefits.

***Ability Score Increase.*** Increase your Strength score by 1, to a maximum of 20.

***Defender.*** You gain a +1 bonus to your Armor Class while you are wielding a Shield.

***Wide Guard.*** As a Bonus Action, you can give any ally the benefits of Half Cover until the start of your next turn while it is within 5 feet of you, provided you have a Shield equipped. If you do, your Speed is halved until the end of your next turn.
