# Vanguard
*Specialization Feat (Prerequisites: Level 8+, Strength or Dexterity 15+, Sentinel)*

You gain the following benefits.

***Ability Score Increase.*** Increase your Strength or Dexterity score by 1, to a maximum of 20.

***High Alert.*** You can take an additional Reaction on each round of combat, but only to make Opportunity Attacks and you can’t take more than one Reaction per turn.

***Vengeful.*** You make Opportunity Attacks with Advantage as long as you haven’t taken damage since your last turn.
