# Skirmisher
*Specialization Feat (Prerequisites: Level 8+, Medium Armor Master)*

You gain the following benefits.

***Ability Score Increase.*** Increase your Dexterity or Strength score by 1, to a maximum of 20.

***Maneuverable.*** Wearing Medium armor doesn’t impose Disadvantage on your Stealth checks.

***Nimble Warrior.*** When you take the Disengage action on your turn while you are wearing Medium armor, you can make one attack using a Melee weapon against a creature as part of that action, provided the target for that attack is within 5 feet of you when you take the action.
