# Dwarven Fortitude
*Racial Feat (Prerequisite: Dwarf, Level 4+)*  

You have the blood of dwarf heroes flowing through your veins. You gain the following benefits.

***Ability Score Increase.*** Increase your Constitution score by 1, to a maximum of 20.

***Dwarven Fortitude.*** When you take the Dodge action, you can expend one of your Hit Point Dice, roll the die, and regain a number of Hit Points equal to the roll plus your Constitution modifier.

# Dwarven Fortitude
*Prerequisite: Dwarf*

You have the blood of dwarf heroes flowing through your veins. You gain the following benefits:

Increase your Constitution score by 1, to a maximum of 20.

Whenever you take the Dodge action in combat, you can spend one Hit Die to heal yourself. Roll the die, add your Constitution modifier, and regain a number of hit points equal to the total (minimum of 1).
