# Improved Sacred Weapon
*Paladin Feat (Prerequisite: Level 4+, Sacred Weapon Feature)*

You gain the following benefits.  
***Ability Score Increase.*** Increase your Strength, Dexterity or Charisma by 1, to a maximum of 20.  
***Improved Sacred Weapon.*** When you hit a target with an attack roll using your Sacred Weapon, the target takes an extra 1d8 Radiant damage if it is a Fiend or an Undead.  
***Repeatable.*** You can take this feat more than once.
