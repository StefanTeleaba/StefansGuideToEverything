# Sacred Aura
*Paladin Feat (Prerequisite: Level 8+, Sacred Weapon Feature, Aura of Protection Feature)*

You gain the following benefits.  
***Ability Score Increase.*** Increase your Strength, Dexterity or Charisma by 1, to a maximum of 20.  
***Sacred Aura.*** While your Sacred Weapon feature is active, your allies can add your Charisma modifier to attack rolls if they are within your Aura of Protection.
