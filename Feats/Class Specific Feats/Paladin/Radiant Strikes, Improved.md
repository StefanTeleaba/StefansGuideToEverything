# Improved Radiant Strikes
*Paladin Feat (Prerequisite: Level 12+, Radiant Strikes Feature)*

You gain the following benefits.  
***Ability Score Increase.*** Increase your Strength, Dexterity or Charisma by 1, to a maximum of 20.  
***Improved Radiant Strikes.*** The damage of your Radiant Strikes increases by 1d8 if the target is a Fiend or an Undead.  
***Repeatable.*** You can take this feat more than once.
