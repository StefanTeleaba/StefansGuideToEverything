# Improved Faithful Steed
*Paladin Feat (Prerequisite: Level 8+, Faithful Steed Feature)*

You gain the following benefits.  
***Ability Score Increase.*** Increase your Constitution or Charisma by 1, to a maximum of 20.  
***Improved Faithful Steed.*** When you cast the Find Steed spell, treat the spell as if you had cast it using a spell slot two levels higher than the spell slot you used.  
***Repeatable.*** You can take this feat more than once.
