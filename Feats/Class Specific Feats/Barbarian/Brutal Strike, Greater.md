# Greater Brutal Strike
*Barbarian Feat (Prerequisite: Level 12+, Brutal Strike Feature)*

You gain the following benefits.  
***Ability Score Increase.*** Increase your Strength score by 1, to a maximum of 20.  
***Greater Brutal Strike.*** The extra damage of your Brutal Strike increases by 1d10. In addition, you can use one more Brutal Strike effects whenever you use your Brutal Strike feature.  
***Repeatable.*** You can take this feat more than once.
