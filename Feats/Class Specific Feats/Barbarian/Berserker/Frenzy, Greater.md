# Greater Frenzy
*Barbarian Feat (Prerequisite: Level 4+, Frenzy Feature)*

You gain the following benefits.  
***Ability Score Increase.*** Increase your Strength score by 1, to a maximum of 20.  
***Greater Frenzy.*** If you use Reckless Attack while your Rage is active, you deal extra damage with Strength-based attacks. To determine the extra damage, roll a number of d6s equal to your Rage Damage bonus, and add them together. The damage has the same type as the weapon or Unarmed Strike used for the attack.
