# Greater Power of the Wilds
*Barbarian Feat (Prerequisite: Level 16+, Improved Power of the Wilds Feat)*

You gain the following benefits.  
***Ability Score Increase.*** Increase your Strength, Dexterity, Constitution or Wisdom score by 1, to a maximum of 20.  
***Greater Power of the Wilds.*** Whenever you activate your Rage, you gain all three options available for your Power of the Wilds feature.
