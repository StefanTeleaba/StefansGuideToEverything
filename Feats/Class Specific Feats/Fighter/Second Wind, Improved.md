# Improved Second Wind
*Class Feat (Prerequisite: Level 4+, Second Wind Feature)*

You gain the following benefits.  
***Ability Score Increase.*** Increase your Constitution by 1, to a maximum of 20.  
***Improved Second Wind.*** When you use your Second Wind feature, you can add your Fighter Level one more time to the number of Hit Points you regain.  
***Repeatable.*** You can take this feat more than once.
