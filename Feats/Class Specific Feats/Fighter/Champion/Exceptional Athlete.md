# Exceptional Athlete
*General Feat (Prerequisite: Level 4+, Remarkable Athlete Feature)*

You gain the following benefits.  
***Ability Score Increase.*** Increase your Strength, Dexterity or Constitution score by 1, to a maximum of 20.  
***Initiative Proficiency.*** When you roll Initiative, you can add your Proficiency Bonus to the roll.  
***Exceptional Athlete.*** If you lack proficiency with Athletics, you gain proficiency in it, and if you already have proficiency in it, you gain Expertise in it. In addition, immediately after you score a Critical Hit, you can move up to your Speed without provoking Opportunity Attacks.
