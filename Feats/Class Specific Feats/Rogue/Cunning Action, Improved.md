# Improved Cunning Action
*Rogue Feat (Prerequisite: Level 4+, Cunning Action Feature)*

*Your quick thinking and agility allow you to move and act more quickly.*

You gain the following benefits.  
***Ability Score Increase.*** Increase your Dexterity or Intelligence score by 1, to a maximum of 20.  
***Improved Cunning Action.*** You have an additional action on each of your turns. That action can be used to take only the Dash, Disengage, Hide, or Utilize action.
