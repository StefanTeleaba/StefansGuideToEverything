# Improved Assassinate
*Rogue Feat (Prerequisite: Level 4+, Assassinate Feature)*

You gain the following benefits.  
***Ability Score Increase.*** Increase one ability score of your choice by 1, to a maximum of 20.  
***Improved Surprizing Strikes.*** When you deal Sneak Attack damage during the first round of each combat, you can add your Rogue Level one more time to that damage.  
***Repeatable.*** You can take this feat more than once.
