# Extra Channel Divinity
*Paladin Feat (Prerequisite: Level 4+, Channel Divinity Feature)*

You gain the following benefits.  
***Ability Score Increase.*** Increase your Charisma modifier by 1, to a maximum of 20.  
***Extra Channel Divinity.*** The number of times you can use your Channel Divinity feature increases by 2.  
***Repeatable.*** You can take this feat more than once.

*Homebrew*
