# Expanded Aura of Protection
*Paladin Feat (Prerequisite: Level 8+, Aura of Protection Feature)*

You gain the following benefits.  
***Ability Score Increase.*** Increase your Charisma by 1, to a maximum of 20.  
***Expanded Aura of Protection.*** The Emanation of your Aura of Protection increases by 10 feet. When you reach 18 levels in the Paladin class, the size of this bonus increases to 30 feet.  
***Repeatable.*** You can take this feat more than once.
