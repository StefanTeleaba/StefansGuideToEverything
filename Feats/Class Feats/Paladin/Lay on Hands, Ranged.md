# Ranged Lay on Hands
*Paladin Feat (Prerequisite: Level 4+, Lay on Hands Feature)*

You gain the following benefits.  
***Ability Score Increase.*** Increase your Constitution or Charisma by 1, to a maximum of 20.  
***Ranged Lay on Hands.*** When you use your Lay on Hands, you can now target any creature within your Aura of Protection.
