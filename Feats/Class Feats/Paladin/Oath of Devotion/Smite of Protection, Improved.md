# Improved Smite of Protection
*Paladin Feat (Prerequisite: Level 16+, Smite of Protection Feature)*

You gain the following benefits.  
***Ability Score Increase.*** Increase one ability score of your choice by 1, to a maximum of 20.  
***Improved Smite of Protection.*** Whenever you cast Divine Smite, you and your allies now have Three-Quarters Cover while in your Aura of Protection.
