# Improved Aura of Protection
*Paladin Feat (Prerequisite: Level 8+, Aura of Protection Feature)*

You gain the following benefits.  
***Ability Score Increase.*** Increase your Charisma by 1, to a maximum of 20.  
***Improved Aura of Protection.*** You and your allies have Advantage on saving throws while in your Aura of Protection.
