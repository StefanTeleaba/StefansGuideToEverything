# Greater Aura of Protection
*Paladin Feat (Prerequisite: Level 8+, Improved Aura of Protection Feat)*

You gain the following benefits.  
***Ability Score Increase.*** Increase your Charisma by 1, to a maximum of 20.  
***Greater Aura of Protection.*** If you or an ally in your Aura of Protection fail a saving throw, you can expend one use of your Channel Divinity to reroll that save with a bonus equal to your Paladin level.
