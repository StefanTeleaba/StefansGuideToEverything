# Swift Channel Divinity
*Paladin Feat (Prerequisite: Level 4+, Channel Divinity Feature)*

You gain the following benefits.  
***Ability Score Increase.*** Increase your Charisma modifier by 1, to a maximum of 20.  
***Extra Channel Divinity.*** You now regain all your expended uses of Channel Divinity when you finish a Short or Long Rest.
