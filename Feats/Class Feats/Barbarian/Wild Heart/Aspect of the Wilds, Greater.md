# Greater Aspect of the Wilds
*Barbarian Feat (Prerequisite: Level 8+, Improved Aspect of the Wilds Feat)*

You gain the following benefits.  
***Ability Score Increase.*** Increase your Strength, Dexterity, Constitution or Wisdom score by 1, to a maximum of 20.  
***Greater Aspect of the Wilds.*** You gain all three options of your Aspects of the Wilds feature.
