# Improved Rage of the Wilds
*Barbarian Feat (Prerequisite: Level 4+, Rage of the Wilds Feature)*

You gain the following benefits.  
***Ability Score Increase.*** Increase your Strength, Dexterity, Constitution or Wisdom score by 1, to a maximum of 20.  
***Improved Rage of the Wilds.*** Whenever you activate your Rage, you can choose to gain two of the options available for your Rage of the Wilds feature.
