# Extra Action Surge
*Class Feat (Prerequisite: Level 4+, Action Surge Feature)*

***Ability Score Increase.*** Increase your Strength, Dexterity or Constitution score by 1, to a maximum of 20.  
***Action Surge.*** You gain one more use of your Action Surge feature.  
***Repeatable.*** You can take this feat more than once.
