# Improved Tactical Shift
*Class Feat (Prerequisite: Level 6+, Tactical Shift Feature)*

You gain the following benefits.  
***Ability Score Increase.*** Increase your Strength, Dexterity or Constitution score by 1, to a maximum of 20.  
***Improved Tactical Shift.*** Whenever you activate your Second Wind with a Bonus Action, you can move up to your Speed without provoking Opportunity Attacks.
