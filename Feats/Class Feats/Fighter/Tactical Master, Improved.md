# Improved Tactical Master
*Class Feat (Prerequisite: Level 12+, Tactical Master Feature)*

You gain the following benefits.  
***Ability Score Increase.*** Increase one ability score of your choice by 1, to a maximum of 20.  
***Improved Tactical Master.*** When you attack with a weapon whose mastery property you can use, you can activate the Push, Sap, or Slow mastery property for that attack in addition to the mastery property of that weapon.
