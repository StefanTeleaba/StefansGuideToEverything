# Improved Countercharm
*Paladin Feat (Prerequisite: Level 8+, Countercharm Feature)*

You gain the following benefits.  
***Ability Score Increase.*** Increase your Charisma by 1, to a maximum of 20.  
***Improved Countercharm.*** When you use your Countercharm feature, the creature gains a bonus to the reroll equal to your Performance check modifier.

*Homebrew*
