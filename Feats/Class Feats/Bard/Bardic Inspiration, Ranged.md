# Ranged Bardic Inspiration
*Bard Feat (Prerequisite: Level 4+, Bardic Inspiration Feature)*

You gain the following benefits.  
***Ability Score Increase.*** Increase your Charisma by 1, to a maximum of 20.  
***Ranged Bardic Inspiration.*** The Range of your *Bardic Inspiration* increases by 60 feet. This increase also applies to features like *Countercharm* and *Cutting Words*.  
***Repeatable.*** You can take this feat more than once.
