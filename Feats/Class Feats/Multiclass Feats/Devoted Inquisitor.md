# Devoted Inquisitor
*Class Feat (Prerequisite: Level 4+, Paladin's Smite Feature, Sneak Attack Feature)*

*Your faithful service to your patron deity involves training and methods that many paladins consider questionable. By using the unconventional methods of rogues and assassins, you have learned to deliver devastating sneak attacks against evil foes.*

***Ability Score Increase.*** Increase your Dexterity or Charisma by 1, to a maximum of 20.  
***Dazing Strike.*** When you deal Sneak Attack damage and Divine Smite damage on the same attack, the target can only move or take an Action or a Bonus Action on its next turn.
