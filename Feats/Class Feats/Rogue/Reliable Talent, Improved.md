# Improved Reliable Talent
*Rogue Feat (Prerequisite: Level 8+, Reliable Talent Feature)*

You gain the following benefits.  
***Ability Score Increase.*** Increase one ability score of your choice by 1, to a maximum of 20.  
***Improved Reliable Talent.*** Whenever you make an ability check that uses one of your skills in which you have Expertise, you can treat a d20 roll of 14 or lower as a 15.
