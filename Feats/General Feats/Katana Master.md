# Katana Master
*General Feat (Prerequisite: Level 4+, Proficiency with the Katana)*

You have trained in the ways of the Katana. You gain the following benefits.  
***Ability Score Increase.*** You increase your Strength or Dexterity score by 1, to a maximum of 20.  
***Katana Master.*** The Katana gains the *Light* property and the *Nick* mastery while you wield it and can deal your choice of Piercing or Slashing damage.
