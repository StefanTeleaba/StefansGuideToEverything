# Improved Toughness
*General Feat (Prerequisite: Level 4+)*

You gain the following benefits.  
***Ability Score Increase.*** Increase your Constitution score by 1, to a maximum of 20.  
***Improved Toughness.*** Your Hit Point maximum increases by an amount equal to twice your character level when you gain this feat. Whenever you gain a character level thereafter, your Hit Point maximum increases by an additional 2 Hit Points.  
***Repeatable.*** You can take this feat more than once.
