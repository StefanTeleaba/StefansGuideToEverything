# Burning Blood
*Level 4 Necromancy (Sorcerer, Wizard)*

**Casting Time:** Action  
**Range:** 60 feet  
**Components:** V, S  
**Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute

You turn the blood of a creature within range to flame, driving it berserk with pain. The target takes 6d4 Fire damage and 6d4 Acid damage. At the start of each of its turns until the spell ends, the target takes 3d4 Fire damage and 3d4 Acid damage, it must use its action before moving to make an attack against the closest creature to itself, and then makes a Constitution saving throw. On a failed save, the spell continues. On a successful save, the spell ends.

***Using a Higher-Level Spell Slot.*** The damage increases by 2d4 for each spell slot level above 1.
