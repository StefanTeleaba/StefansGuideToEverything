# Lesser Restoration
*Level 2 Abjuration (Bard, Cleric, Druid, Paladin, Ranger)*

**Casting Time:** Bonus Action  
**Range:** Touch  
**Components:** V, S  
**Duration:** Instantaneous

You touch a creature and end one condition on it: Blinded, Deafened, Paralyzed, or Poisoned.
