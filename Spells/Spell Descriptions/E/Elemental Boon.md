## Elemental Boon
*Level 1 Transmutation (Ranger)*

***Casting Time:*** Bonus Action  
***Range:*** Self  
***Components:*** V, S  
***Duration:*** 1 minute

Choose Acid, Cold, Fire, Lightning, Poison or Thunder. Until the spell ends, your attacks with weapons or Unarmed Strikes deal an extra 1d4 damage of the chosen type on a hit.

***Using a Higher-Level Spell Slot.*** The damage increases by 1d4 for every two spell slot levels above 1.

*Homebrew*  
*Based on Divine Favor*
