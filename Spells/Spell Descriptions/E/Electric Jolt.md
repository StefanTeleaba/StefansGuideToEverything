# Electric Jolt
*Evocation Cantrip (Sorcerer, Wizard)*

**Casting Time:** Action  
**Range:** 30 feet  
**Components:** V, S  
**Duration:** Instantaneous

*A white-hot electric spark dances on your fingertip and then bolts toward your target.*

Make a ranged spell attack against a creature you can see within range. On a hit, the target takes 1d8 Lightning damage. If the target is soaked in a conductive liquid or wearing metal armor, you have Advantage on the attack roll.

***Cantrip Upgrade.*** The damage increases by 1d8 when you reach 5th level(2d8), 11th level(3d8), and 17th level(4d8).
