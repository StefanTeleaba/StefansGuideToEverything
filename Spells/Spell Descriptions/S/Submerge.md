# Submerge
*Level 4 Conjuration (Druid, Sorcerer, Wizard)*

**Casting Time:** Action  
**Range:** Touch  
**Components:** V, S  
**Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute

Water fills the airways of a creature that you try to touch. Make a melee spell attack against the target. On a hit, the target runs out of breath and begins to suffocate for the duration unless it can breathe underwater.

The spell ends for an affected creature if the water is forced out or removed, which can be done in the following ways:

The creature can take an action to make a Constitution check against your spell save DC. On a success, the creature forces the water out of itself.  
Another creature within 5 feet of the target can take an action to make a Wisdom (Medicine) check against your spell save DC. On a success, it forces the water out of the target.  
If the creature is targeted by the Destroy Water option of the Create or Destroy Water spell, the water is automatically removed.

***Using a Higher-Level Spell Slot.*** You can target one additional creature for each spell slot level above 4.
