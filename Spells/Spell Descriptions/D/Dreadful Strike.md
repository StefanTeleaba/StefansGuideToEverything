# Dreadful Strike
*Enchantment Cantrip (Bard, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard)*

**Casting Time:** Action  
**Range:** Self  
**Components:** S, M (a weapon with which you have proficiency and that is worth 1+ CP)  
**Duration:** Instantaneous

You make one attack with the weapon used in the spell's casting. The attack uses your spellcasting ability for the attack and damage rolls instead of using Strength or Dexterity. If the attack deals damage, it can be Psychic damage or the weapon's normal damage type (your choice).

***Cantrip Upgrade.*** Whether you deal Psychic damage or the weapon's normal damage type, the attack deals extra Psychic damage when you reach levels 5 (1d6), 11 (2d6), and 17 (3d6).

# Dreadful Strike
*Enchantment Cantrip (Bard, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard)*

**Casting Time:** Action  
**Range:** Self  
**Components:** S, M (a weapon with which you have proficiency and that is worth 1+ CP)  
**Duration:** Instantaneous

You make one attack with the weapon used in the spell's casting. The attack uses your spellcasting ability for the attack and damage rolls instead of using Strength or Dexterity. If the attack hits, the target is covered in glowing specks of dust until the start of your next turn. If the target makes an attack or casts a spell before then, the target takes 1d8 Psychic damage and the spell ends.

***Cantrip Upgrade.*** At 5th level, the attack deals an extra 1d8 Psychic damage to the target on a hit, and the damage the target takes for making an attack or casting a spell increases to 2d8. Both damage rolls increase by 1d8 at 11th level (2d8 and 3d8) and again at 17th level (3d8 and 4d8).
