# Waterspout
*Level 2 Transmutation (Druid)*

**Casting Time:** Action  
**Range:** 120 feet  
**Components:** V, S  
**Duration:** 1 round

You cause a pillar of water to burst from the ground at a point you can see within range. The pillar is a Cylinder, 5 feet wide and up to 30 feet high. The ground must be capable of supporting the pillar's diameter.

If you create the pillar under a Medium or smaller creature, that creature must make a Dexterity Saving Throw. A creature can choose to fail this save. On a failed save, the creature is lifted to the top of the pillar. On a successful save, the creature moves to the nearest unoccupied space adjacent to the pillar.

If the pillar is prevented from reaching its full height by a ceiling or other permanent obstacle, a creature atop the pillar takes 3d6 bludgeoning damage and has the Restrained condition, pinched between the water and the obstacle. A restrained creature can use its Action to make a Strength or Dexterity check against your Spell Save DC. On a success, the creature is no longer Restrained and must immediately move or fall into an adjacent space.

The pillar remains until the start of your next turn, at which point the water vanishes. Any creature or object still supported by the pillar falls unless it has a flying speed.

A successful athletics check versus your spell save DC is required to move through the space that the waterspout occupies.

***At Higher Levels.*** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, you create one additional pillar for each slot level above 2nd. A creature can only be affected by one pillar at a time.
