# Water Bolt
*Conjuration Cantrip (Druid, Sorcerer, Wizard)*

**Casting Time:** Action  
**Range:** 90 feet  
**Components:** S, M (a filled water skin)  
**Duration:** Instantaneous

You fire a stream of water at one creature or object within range. Make a ranged spell attack against the target. On a hit, the target takes 1d6 Bludgeoning damage, and if it is Large or smaller it is pushed 5 feet directly away from you.

Before rolling damage for the spell, you can forgo rolling one or more d6s, to a maximum amount equal to your spellcasting modifier. For each d6 you forgo, the target is pushed an additional 5 feet.

***Cantrip Upgrade.*** The damage increases by 1d6 when you reach levels 5 (2d6), 11 (3d6), and 17 (4d6).
