# Air Breathing
*Level 1 Transmutation (Artificer, Druid, Ranger, Sorcerer, Wizard)*

**Casting Time:** Action or Ritual  
**Range:** Touch  
**Components:** V, S, M (a water-filled bowl)  
**Duration:** 24 Hours

This spell grants a willing creature that you touch the ability to breathe air until the spell ends. The creature also retains its normal mode of respiration. For example, a shark affected by this spell would also be able to breathe air for the duration.
