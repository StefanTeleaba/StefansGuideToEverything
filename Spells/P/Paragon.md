## Paragon
*Level 6 Transmutation (Sorcerer, Wizard)*

**Casting Time:** Action  
**Range:** Touch  
**Components:** V, S  
**Duration:** 8 hours  

Choose three ability scores. The target scores are set to 20 for the duration of the spell. The spell has no effect on a score if it is 20 or higher already.
