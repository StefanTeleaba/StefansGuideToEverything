# Silver Slinger
*Weapon (Heavy Crossbow), Uncommon*

*The crossbow was carved in the form of a beaked, horned beast, with wings outstretched serving as the prods and bolts shooting from its mouth. The crossbow magically transmuted bolts fired from it into blunt silver bolts, better suited for harming undead.*

You gain a +1 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls made with this magic weapon.

A bolt fired from this weapon deals Bludgeoning damage instead of Piercing damage on a hit, and deals one additional die of damage when you score a Critical Hit against a creature that is shape-shifted.

*Homebrew*

# Silver Slinger
*Weapon (Heavy Crossbow), Legendary*

*The crossbow was carved in the form of a beaked, horned beast, with wings outstretched serving as the prods and bolts shooting from its mouth. The crossbow magically transmuted bolts fired from it into blunt silver bolts, better suited for harming undead.*

You gain a +1 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. The bonus increases to +3 when you use the weapon to attack an Undead.

A bolt fired from this weapon deals Bludgeoning damage instead of Piercing damage on a hit, and deals one additional die of damage when you score a Critical Hit against a creature that is shape-shifted.

When you score a Critical Hit with this weapon, the target takes an extra 10 Bludgeoning damage.

When you hit an Undead with this magic weapon, that creature takes an extra 2d10 Radiant damage. If the target has 25 Hit Points or fewer after taking this damage, it must succeed on a DC 15 Wisdom saving throw or be destroyed. On a successful save, the creature has the Frightened condition until the end of your next turn.

*Homebrew*
