# Myia, the Silent Huntress
*Weapon (Shortbow), Uncommon*

You gain a +1 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls made with this weapon.

Attacks you make with this weapon are completely silent. If you make an attack roll while hidden and the roll misses, making the attack roll doesn't reveal your location.

When you deal Skeak Attack damage, you mark the creature until the end of your next turn. Your Critical Range against a marked creature increases by 1.

# Myia, the Silent Huntress
*Weapon (Shortbow), Rare*

You gain a +2 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls made with this weapon.

Attacks you make with this weapon are completely silent. If you make an attack roll while hidden and the roll misses, making the attack roll doesn't reveal your location.

When you deal Skeak Attack damage, you mark the creature until the end of your next turn. Your Critical Range against a marked creature increases by 1.

On a Critical Hit, the target must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw (DC 14) or have the Deafened contition for 1 minute. While Deafened, the creature is also unable to speak. The target repeats the save at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on itself on a success.
