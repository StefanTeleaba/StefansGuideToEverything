# Firefiend
*Weapon (Great Scimitar), Rare*

You gain a +2 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls made with this magic weapon.

When you take the Attack action on your turn and attack with this weapon, you can make one extra attack as part of that action. That extra attack must be made with the same weapon.

When you take the Attack action, you can use *Firefiend* to self-immolate. Golden flames extend from Firefiend and cover your entire body. For 1 minute, you gain the following benefits.
- Attacks with Firefiend deal an extra 1d6 Fire damage.
- You shed Bright Light in a 10-foot radius and Dim Light for an additional 10 feet.
- You have Immunity to Cold damage.
- Whenever a creature hits you with a melee attack roll, it takes 2d8 Fire damage.
