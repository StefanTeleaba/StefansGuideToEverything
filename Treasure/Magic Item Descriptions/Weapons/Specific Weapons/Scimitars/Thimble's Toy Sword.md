# Thimble's Toy Sword
*Weapon (Scimitar), Uncommon*

*This toy longsword was a gift from Ulbid Morn, who made it for his daughter Cerebel.*

You gain a +1 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls made with this magic weapon.  

*Attuned to:*  
***Bane.*** This weapon deals an additional 2d6 damage on a Hit against incorporeal foes.
