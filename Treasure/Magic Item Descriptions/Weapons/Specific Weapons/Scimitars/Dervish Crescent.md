# Dervish Crescent
*Weapon (Scimitar), Very Rare*

*The length of this blade is etched with flames, genies, and whirlwinds. Small glittering particles along its length evoke a sandstorm. The style and quality of the blade indicates it was made in Calimshan.*

You gain a +3 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls made with this magic weapon.

When you reduce an enemy to 0 Hit Points while wielding this weapon, you gain the effects of the *Fire Shield (warm)* spell. You also gain this benefit if someone else reduces an enemy within 10 feet of you to 0 Hit Points. The spell ends if you are no longer holding the weapon.
