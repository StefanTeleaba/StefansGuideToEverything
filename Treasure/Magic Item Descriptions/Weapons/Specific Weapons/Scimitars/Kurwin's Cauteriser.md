# Ambusher
*Weapon (Scimitar), Uncommon*

*Mortician Kurwin was known to use his trusty Cauteriser on all sorts of interesting subjects. At first there were cadavers, then people who wouldn't be missed, and it was only after he targeted a well-liked tavern owner that he was ratted out to the law.*

You gain a +1 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls made with this magic weapon.  
***Flaming.*** This weapon deals an additional 1d6 Fire damage on a Hit.  
***Cauterizer.*** On a Hit, the target must succeed on a Dexterity saving throw (DC 8 plus your Dexterity modifier and Proficiency Bonus) or start Burning.


**Source:** *Baldur's Gate 3*
