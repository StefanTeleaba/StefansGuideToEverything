# Speedy Reply
*Weapon (Scimitar), Uncommon*

*A heavy haft and wide blade belie the speed of this weapon. It parts the air as swiftly as any dueling sabre.*

You gain a +1 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls made with this magic weapon.

***Nimble Attack.*** When you hit a creature with an attack using this magic weapon, your Speed increases by 10 feet until the start of your next turn.

# Speedy Reply
*Weapon (Scimitar), Rare*

*A heavy haft and wide blade belie the speed of this weapon. It parts the air as swiftly as any dueling sabre.*

You gain a +2 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls made with this magic weapon.

***Nimble Attack.*** When you hit a creature with an attack using this magic weapon, your Speed increases by 10 feet until the start of your next turn.

***Retaliation.*** When you take damage from a creature that is within 5 feet of you, you can take a Reaction to make one melee attack against that creature, using this weapon.
