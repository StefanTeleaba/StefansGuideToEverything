# Templar's Retribution
*Weapon (Bastard Sword), Uncommon*

*The blade of this sword was made of cold iron.*

You gain a +1 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls made with this magic weapon.

When you score a Critical Hit with this weapon against a Fey creature, the weapon deals one additional die of damage.  

When you score a Critical Hit with this weapon against a Fiend or Undead creature, the weapon deals an additional 1d10 Radiant damage.

# Templar's Retribution
*Weapon (Bastard Sword), Rare*

*The blade of this sword was made of cold iron.*

You gain a +2 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls made with this magic weapon.

When you score a Critical Hit with this weapon against a Fey creature, the weapon deals one additional die of damage.  

When you hit a Fiend or an Undead with this magic weapon, that creature takes an extra 1d10 Radiant damage.

# Templar's Retribution
*Weapon (Bastard Sword), Very Rare*

*The blade of this sword was made of cold iron.*

You gain a +3 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls made with this magic weapon.

When you score a Critical Hit with this weapon against a Fey creature, the weapon deals one additional die of damage.  

This weapon deals an additional 1d10 Radiant damage on a Hit. If the target has 25 Hit Points or fewer after taking this damage, it must succeed on a DC 15 Wisdom saving throw or be destroyed. On a successful save, the creature has the Frightened condition until the end of your next turn.
