# Red Death
*Weapon (Bastard Sword), Legendary*

*Red Death was an intimidating weapon that was formed from crimson marble by using a stone shape spell. Its sharp edge was polished and strengthened with silver, and its hilt was swathed in black dragon hide.*

You gain a +4 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls made with this magic weapon.  

When you score a Critical Hit with this weapon against a creature that is shape-shifted, the weapon deals two additional dice of damage.  

You can use Red Death to cast one of the following spells: *Comprehend Languages*, *Detect Magic*, *Detect Evil and Good*, *Enhance Ability (Strength)*, *Heal* and *Telepathy*. Once Red Death has been used to cast a spell, it can't be used to cast that spell again until the next dawn. The spells use your spellcasting ability and spell save DC.
