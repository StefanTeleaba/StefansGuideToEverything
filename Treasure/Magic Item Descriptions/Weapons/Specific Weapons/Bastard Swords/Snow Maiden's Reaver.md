# Snow Maiden's Reaver
*Weapon (Bastard Sword), Uncommon*

*The enchanted bastard sword was created out of magic ice with only its grip and pommel being made out of silver and steel.*

You gain a +1 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls made with this magic weapon.

Whenever you roll a 20 on your attack roll with this weapon, the target becomes a frozen statue until it thaws. A creature is immune to this effect if it has Immunity to Cold damage. If the creature has Legendary Resistance, it can expend one daily use of that trait to avoid freezing.

# Snow Maiden's Reaver
*Weapon (Bastard Sword), Rare*

*The enchanted bastard sword was created out of magic ice with only its grip and pommel being made out of silver and steel.*

You gain a +2 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. In addition, the weapon ignores Resistance to Cold damage.

This weapon deals an additional 1d10 Cold damage on a Hit.

Whenever you roll a 20 on your attack roll with this weapon, the target becomes a frozen statue until it thaws. A creature is immune to this effect if it has Immunity to Cold damage. If the creature has Legendary Resistance, it can expend one daily use of that trait to avoid freezing.
