# Discord
*Weapon (Bastard Sword), Very Rare*

You gain a +3 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls made with this magic weapon.  

This weapon deals an additional 1d10 Thunder damage on a Hit.

On a Critical Hit, each creature of your choice within 5 feet of the target takes 2d10 Thunder damage.

While the sword is on your person, you can cast *Thunder Step* (Level 5, DC 17) from it. The sword can't cast this spell again until the next dawn.
