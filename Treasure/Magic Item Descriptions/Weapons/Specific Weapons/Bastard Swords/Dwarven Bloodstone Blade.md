# Dwarven Bloodstone Blade
*Weapon (Bastard Sword), Uncommon*

*Thie weapon is decorated with bloodstones.*

You gain a +1 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. This bonus increases to +3 against Giants and the weapon deals an additional 1d10 damage.

The Critical Range of this weapon increases by 1.
