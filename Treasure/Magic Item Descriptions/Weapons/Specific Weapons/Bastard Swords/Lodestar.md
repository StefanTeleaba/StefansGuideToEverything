# Lodestar
*Weapon (Bastard Sword), Very Rare (Requires Attunement)*

*This sword was forged from the heart of a fallen star. It's blade is as pale as milkglass, alive with light.*

This weapon deals one additional die of damage on a hit and you have Disadvantage on attack rolls with it if your Strength score isn't at least 23. In addition, the weapon ignores Resistance to Slashing damage and has the Thrown property with a normal range of 20 feet and a long range of 60 feet. Immediately after hitting or missing, the weapon flies back to your hand. 

When you let go of the sword, you can take a Utilize action and cause it to become fixed in place. The sword doesn't move, even if it defies gravity and can hold up to 10,000 pounds of weight. More weight causes the sword to fall. A creature can take a Utilize action to make a DC 30 Strength (Athletics) check, moving the fixed sword up to 10 feet on a successful check. If it is on the same plane of existence, you can summon the sword as a Bonus Action, causing it to fly back to your hand at a rate of 500 feet per round.

***Spells.*** While the sword is on your person, you can cast the following spells (save DC 18) from it:
- Gravity Fissure
- Gravity Sinkhole
Once you use the sword to cast a spell, you can't cast that spell again from it until the next dawn.

### Bastard Sword (35 GP)
*Martial Melee Weapon*  

**Damage:** 1d10 Slashing  
**Properties:** Heavy, Versatile (1d12)  
**Mastery:** Sap  
**Weight:** 4 lb.

*Player's Handbook v3.5, p.121*
Bastard swords are also known as hand-and-a-half swords. A bastard sword is too large to use in one hand without special training; thus, it is an exotic weapon. A character can use a bastard sword two-handed as a martial weapon.
