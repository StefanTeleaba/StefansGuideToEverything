# Blackthorn
*Weapon (Club), Uncommon*

*Fashioned from the hard, unforgiving trunk of a blackthorn, this club still retains the perfume of the forest.*

You gain a +1 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls made with this magic weapon.  
***Forest Harmony.*** When you cast Shillelagh on this weapon, it deals an additional 1d6 damage on a hit.


**Source:** *Baldur's Gate 3*
