# Mountainfist
*Weapon (Club), Rare*

*This oversized yet uncannily lightweight club was created using a clutched fist of a petrified ogre.*

You gain a +1 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. This bonus increases to +3 against Contructs.

When you score a Critical Hit with this weapon against a Construct, the target takes an extra 4d6 Bludgeoning damage.If a Construct has 25 Hit Points or fewer after taking this damage, it is destroyed.

Your Strength score is 19 while you hold this weapon. It has no effect on you if your Strength is 19 or higher without it.
