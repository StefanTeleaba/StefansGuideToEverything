# Mighty Oak
*Weapon (Club), Uncommon*

*Fashioned from the hardest old oak from the High Forest, this gnarled cudgel can fell an adult grizzly when wielded by a skilled warrior.*

You gain a +1 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. When you cast *Shillelagh* on this weapon, this bonus increases to +2. In addition, you can use the club as a Spellcasting Focus for your Druid and Ranger spells.
