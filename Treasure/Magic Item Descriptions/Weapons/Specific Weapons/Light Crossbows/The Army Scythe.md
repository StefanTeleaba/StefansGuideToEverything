# The Army Scythe
*Weapon (Light Crossbow), Uncommon*

*Bolts slot into this crossbow with wonderful ease, as if eager to nestle against the stock, soon to fly.*

You gain a +1 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls made with this magic weapon and you can ignore its Loading property.

# The Army Scythe
*Weapon (Light Crossbow), Rare*

*Bolts slot into this crossbow with wonderful ease, as if eager to nestle against the stock, soon to fly.*

You gain a +2 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls made with this magic weapon and you can ignore its Loading property.

# The Army Scythe
*Weapon (Light Crossbow), Very Rare*

*Bolts slot into this crossbow with wonderful ease, as if eager to nestle against the stock, soon to fly.*

You gain a +3 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls made with this magic weapon and you can ignore its Loading property.

When you take the Attack action on your turn and attack with this weapon, you can make one extra attack as part of that action. That extra attack must be made with the same weapon.

As an Action, you brandish the weapon and conjure spectral bolts that launch forward and then disappear. Each creature of your choice that you can see in a 60-foot Cone makes a Dexterity saving throw (DC 17), taking 7d8 Force damage on a failed save or half as much damage on a successful one. This action can't be used again until the next dawn.
