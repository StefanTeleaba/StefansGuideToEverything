# Eye of the Storm
*Weapon (Rapier), Rare*

You gain a +1 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls made with this magic weapon.

While you hold this weapon, you have Resistance to Lightning and Thunder damage.

On a Critical Hit, the target takes an extra 3d8 Thunder damage and is pushed 15 feet away from you.

# Eye of the Storm
*Weapon (Rapier), Very Rare*

You gain a +2 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls made with this magic weapon.

On a Critical Hit, the target takes an extra 3d8 Thunder damage and is pushed 15 feet away from you.

Once on each of your turns, you can leap horizontally up to 30 feet by spending 10 feet of movement. You turn into a bolt of lightning forming a 30-foot-long, 5-foot-wide Line that blasts out from you in a direction you choose. Each creature in the Line takes 1d8 Lightning damage and you reappear at the end of the line.
