# Quick
*Weapon (Rapier), Rare*

*The guard of this fine steel rapier is adorned with a sapphire that answers to the command word "Storm's lash!"*

You gain a +2 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls made with this magic weapon.

While holding this magic weapon, you can use the command word "Storm's lash!" to cause lightning to infuse the weapon (no action required). While the weapon is alight, it deals an extra 2d8 Lightning damage on a hit. The lightning lasts until you issue the command again or until you drop, stow, or sheathe the weapon.
