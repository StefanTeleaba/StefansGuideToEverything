# Bottled Water
*Weapon (Whip), Rare*

This clear glass bottle holds a self-replenishing essence of living water that can provide up to 1 gallon of fresh drinking water each hour when drunk from or poured from the bottle. While you’re attuned to the bottle, you can command the water to flow from it and coil around your arm, or to return to the bottle (no action required). While coiled around your arm, the water forms a magic whip that you wield in that hand.

***Flow.*** You gain a +1 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls made with this weapon. In addition, this weapon has a reach of 15 feet rather than the normal reach of a whip, and when you hit with an attack roll using this magic weapon, the target takes an extra 1d4 Cold damage.

***Crack.*** Once per turn, when you take the Attack action, you can replace one of your attacks with a crack of the whip. Choose a point within the whip’s reach. Biting cold water sprays outward from that point in a 30-foot Cone in a direction you choose. Each creature in the Cone must succeed on a DC 15 Strength saving throw, taking 3d8 Cold damage on a failed save or half as much damage on a successful one. On a failed save, a target also has the Prone condition and is pushed up to 15 feet away from the chosen point. You can use this property 3 times, and you regain all expended uses daily at Dawn.
