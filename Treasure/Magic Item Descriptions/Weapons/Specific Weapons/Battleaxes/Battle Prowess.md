# Battle Prowess
*Weapon (Battleaxe), Legendary*

You gain a +3 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls made with this magic weapon.

The Critical Range of this weapon increases by 1.

When you take the Attack action on your turn and attack with this weapon, you can make one extra attack as part of that action. That extra attack must be made with the same weapon.

While holding this weapon, a Lawful creature takes 3d6 Radiant damage at the start of each of its turns.

---
*Faiths and Pantheons v3.5, p.73*

Battle Prowess, the personal weapon of the Faerûnian war god Tempus, was a +3 anarchic keen speed battleaxe.
