# Stormbringer
*Weapon (Battleaxe), Legendary*

*This axe bears the engraving "Lord of Storms".*

You gain a +3 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls made with this magic weapon.

This weapon deals an additional 1d8 Lightning damage on a Hit.  

The weapon has 10 charges. While holding the weapon, you can expend one charge to cast Lightning Bolt (Level 6, DC 19) or two charges to cast Chain Lightning (DC 19) from it. The weapon regains 1d6 + 4 expended charges daily at dawn.

---
Stormbringer was an enchanted battleaxe dedicated to Talos, the Lord of Storms.

The axe was engraved with the words that read "Lord of Storms", a dedication to Talos. However, the engraving bore no magical significance and its +1 enchantment was achieved by some other means. Additionally, the axe dealt magical lightning damage upon successful strike and allowed the wielder to cast the lightning bolt ten times.
