# Axegard
*Weapon (Battleaxe), Legendary*

You gain a +4 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls made with this magic weapon.  

While this weapon is on your person, you gain the following benefits:  
***All-Around Vision.*** The weapon gives you Advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on sight.  
***Special Senses.*** You have Darkvision and Truesight, both with a range of 120 feet.  
***Drawbacks.*** A Light spell cast on the weapon or a Daylight spell cast within 5 feet of the weapon gives you the Blinded condition for 1 minute. At the end of each of your turns, you make a Constitution saving throw (DC 11 for Light or DC 15 for Daylight), ending the condition on yourself on a success.

---

*Axegard was a two-handed battleaxe that was the favored weapon of Gorm Gulthyn, the dwarven deity of vigilance and protection.*
