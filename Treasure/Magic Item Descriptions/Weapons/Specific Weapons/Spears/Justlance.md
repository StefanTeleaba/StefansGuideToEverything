# Justlance
*Weapon (Spear), Uncommon*

*The adamantine tip of this spear is fashioned in the form of a unicorn's horn.*

You gain a +1 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls made with this magic weapon.

Whenever this weapon or piece of ammunition hits an object, the hit is a Critical Hit.

This weapon returns to your hand immediately after you use it to make a ranged attack roll.
