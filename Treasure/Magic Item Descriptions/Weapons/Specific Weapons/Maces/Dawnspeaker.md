# Dawnspeaker
*Weapon (Mace), Legendary*

You gain a +5 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls made with this magic weapon.  
***Disruption.*** If a Fiend or an Undead has 100 Hit Points or fewer after taking damage from this weapon, it must succeed on a DC 21 Wisdom saving throw or be destroyed. On a successful save, the creature has the Frightened condition until the end of your next turn.  
***Ghost Touch.*** This weapon can deal your choice of Force damage or its normal damage type.  
***Holy.*** This weapon deals an additional 1d8 Radiant damage on a Hit.
