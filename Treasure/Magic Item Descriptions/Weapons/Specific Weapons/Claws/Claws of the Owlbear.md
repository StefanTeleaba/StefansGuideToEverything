# Claws of the Owlbear
*Weapon (Claw), Rare*  
*Simple Melee Weapon*  

**Damage:** 1d4 Slashing  
**Properties:** Finesse, Light  
**Mastery:** Nick  
**Weight:** 1 lb.

*Originally belonging to Qawasha, a chultan druid of the Emerald Enclave, these claw weapons were fashioned from the claws of an Owlbear.*

You gain a +2 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls made with this magic weapon.

***Owlbear Shape.*** The *Owlbear* and the *Primeval Owlbear* are added to the Known Forms of your Wild Shape Feature.
