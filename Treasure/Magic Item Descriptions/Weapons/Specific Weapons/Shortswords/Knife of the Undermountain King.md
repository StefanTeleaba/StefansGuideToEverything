*Homebrew*
# Knife of the Undermountain King
*Weapon (Shortsword), Very Rare*

You gain a +2 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls made with this magic weapon.

The Critical Range of this weapon increases by 1 and when you roll a 1 or a 2 on the damage die, you can reroll the die once and use either roll. In addition, you have Advantage on attack rolls against creatures that are entirely in Dim Light or Darkness.

---

*Baldur's Gate 3*

*Snatched from the realm of the mad wizard Halaster Blackcloak, this knife became a prized keepsake of a duergar king.*
