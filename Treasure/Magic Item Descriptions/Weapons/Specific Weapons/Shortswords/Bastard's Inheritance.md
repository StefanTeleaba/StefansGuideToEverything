# Bastard's Inheritance
*Weapon (Shortsword), Uncommon*

You gain a +1 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls made with this magic weapon.

This weapon deals an additional 1d6 Poison damage on a Hit.

# Bastard's Inheritance
*Weapon (Shortsword), Rare*

You gain a +2 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls made with this magic weapon.

On a hit, this weapon deals an additional 1d6 Poison damage and the target has the Poisoned condition until the end of your next turn.
