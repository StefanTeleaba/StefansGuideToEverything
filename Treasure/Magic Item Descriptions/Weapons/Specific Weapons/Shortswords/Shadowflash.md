# Shadowflash
*Weapon (Shortsword), Rare*

*Shadowflash, also known as Silverfalsh, was the gleaming silver shortsword owned by Vhaeraun*

You gain a +1 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls made with this magic weapon.  
***Silver.*** When you score a Critical Hit with this weapon against a creature that is shape-shifted, the weapon deals one additional die of damage.  
***Flash.*** As a Bonus Action, you can launch a flash of silver light. Each creature in a 60-foot Cone originating from you must succeed on a Dexterity saving throw or have the Blinded condition for 1 minute. At the end of each of its turns, the target repeats the save, ending the spell on itself on a success. Once you use this ability, you can't use it again until the next dawn.
