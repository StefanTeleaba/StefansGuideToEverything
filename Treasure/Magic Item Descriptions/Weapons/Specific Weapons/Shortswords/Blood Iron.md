# Blood Iron
*Weapon (Shortsword), Uncommon*

*The sword is sleek and polished, with a blade that absorbs light. A blood-red ruby on the hilt is fitted within a setting of thorns and roses.*

You gain a +1 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls made with this magic weapon.

When you deal damage to a creature with this magic weapon, you regain 1 Hit Point if the target isn't a Construct or an Undead.

# Blood Iron
*Weapon (Shortsword), Very Rare*

*The sword is sleek and polished, with a blade that absorbs light. A blood-red ruby on the hilt is fitted within a setting of thorns and roses.*

You gain a +3 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls made with this magic weapon.

When you deal damage to a creature with this weapon, you regain 1 Hit Point if the target isn't a Construct or an Undead.

When you hit a creature with this weapon, it takes an extra 1d6 Necrotic damage. Necrotic damage you deal ignores Resistance.

When you deal Necrotic damage with this weapon, you can expend and roll one Hit Point Die. You regain Hit Points equal to the number rolled plus the Necrotic damage dealt.

# Blood Iron
*Weapon (Shortsword), Legendary*

*The sword is sleek and polished, with a blade that absorbs light. A blood-red ruby on the hilt is fitted within a setting of thorns and roses.*

You gain a +3 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls made with this magic weapon.

When you deal damage to a creature with this weapon, you regain 1 Hit Point if the target isn't a Construct or an Undead.

When you hit a creature with this weapon, it takes an extra 1d6 Necrotic damage. Necrotic damage you deal ignores Resistance.

When you deal Necrotic damage with this weapon, you can expend and roll one Hit Point Die. You regain Hit Points equal to the number rolled plus the Necrotic damage dealt.

When you score a Critical Hit against a creature, that target takes an extra 15 Necrotic damage if it isn't a Construct or an Undead, and you gain Temporary Hit Points equal to the amount of Necrotic damage dealt.
