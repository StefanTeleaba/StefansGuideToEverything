# Shortsword of First Blood
*Weapon (Shortsword), Rare*

*A golden blade, exquisitely made and astonishingly sharp.*

You gain a +1 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls made with this magic weapon.  
***Break the Unbroken.*** When you hit a creature with this weapon, the weapon deals an extra 1d8 damage to the target if it isn't missing any of its Hit Points.


**Source:** *Baldur's Gate 3*
