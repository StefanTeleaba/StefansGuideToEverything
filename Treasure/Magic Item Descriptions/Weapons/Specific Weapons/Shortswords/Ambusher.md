# Ambusher
*Weapon (Shortsword), Rare*

*This blade is one of a few rare swords wielded by the Eucalyptus League, a group of fabulously wealthy courtesans who owned a great deal of Luskan's bazaars.*

You gain a +1 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls made with this magic weapon.  

*Attuned to:*  
As long as this weapon is within your reach and you are attuned to it, you gain the following benefits.  
***Ambusher.*** During the first round of each combat, this weapon deals an additional 1d6 damage against any creature that hasn't taken a turn.  
***Guardian.*** You gain a +1 bonus to your Initiative rolls if you don't have the Incapacitated condition.  
***Sentinel.*** You have Advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks.


**Source:** *Baldur's Gate 3*
