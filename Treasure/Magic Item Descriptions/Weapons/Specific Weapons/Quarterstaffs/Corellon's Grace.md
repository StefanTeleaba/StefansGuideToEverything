# Corellon's Grace
*Weapon (Quarterstaff), Uncommon*

*This light, flexible staff is of elven make, marked with mithral sigils that channel magic in the blink of an eye.*

You gain a +1 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls made with this magic weapon and with your Unarmed Strikes.

*Attuned to:*  
***Unhampered Resistance.*** As long as this weapon is within your reach and you are attuned to it, you gain a +2 bonus to saving throws if you are wearing no armor and using no Shield.


**Source:** *Baldur's Gate 3*
