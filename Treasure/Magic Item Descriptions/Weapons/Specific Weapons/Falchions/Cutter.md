# Cutter
*Weapon (Falchion), Rare*

You gain a +2 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls made with this magic weapon.

Whenever this weapon or piece of ammunition hits an object, the hit is a Critical Hit.

The Critical Range of this weapon increases by 1.

If you hit a creature with a melee attack roll using this weapon, you can make a melee attack roll with the weapon against a second creature within 5 feet of the first that is also within your reach. On a hit, the second creature takes the weapon's damage, but don't add your ability modifier to that damage unless that modifier is negative. You can make this extra attack only once per turn.

# Cutter
*Weapon (Falchion), Very Rare*

You gain a +3 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls made with this magic weapon.

When you attack an object with this magic weapon and hit, the hit is a Critical Hit and you maximize your weapon damage dice against the target.

The Critical Range of this weapon increases by 1.

If you hit a creature with a melee attack roll using this weapon, you can make a melee attack roll with the weapon against a second creature within 5 feet of the first that is also within your reach. You can make this extra attack only once per turn.
