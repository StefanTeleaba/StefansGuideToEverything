# Blade of Destruction
*Weapon (Falchion), Uncommon*

*Blade of Destruction was a blue tinted heavy two-handed sword that shimmered with magic.*

You gain a +1 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls made with this magic weapon.  

When you score a Critical Hit, the weapon deals one additional die of damage.

# Blade of Destruction
*Weapon (Falchion), Rare*

*Blade of Destruction was a blue tinted heavy two-handed sword that shimmered with magic.*

You gain a +2 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls made with this magic weapon.  

When you score a Critical Hit, you roll all of the attack's damage dice three times instead of two times.

# Blade of Destruction
*Weapon (Falchion), Very Rare*

*Blade of Destruction was a blue tinted heavy two-handed sword that shimmered with magic.*

You gain a +3 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls made with this magic weapon.  

When you score a Critical Hit, you roll all of the attack's damage dice three times instead of two times and you can reroll any 1 or 2 on a damage die.
