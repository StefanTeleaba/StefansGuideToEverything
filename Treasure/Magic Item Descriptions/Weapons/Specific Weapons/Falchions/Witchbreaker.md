# Witchbreaker
*Weapon (Falchion), Uncommon*

You gain a +1 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls made with this magic weapon.  

You have Advantage on attack rolls against creatures that are Concentrating.

# Witchbreaker
*Weapon (Falchion), Rare*

You gain a +2 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls made with this magic weapon.  

You have Advantage on attack rolls against creatures that are Concentrating.

When you hit a creature with this weapon, that creature can't cast spells that include a Verbal component until the end of your next turn.
