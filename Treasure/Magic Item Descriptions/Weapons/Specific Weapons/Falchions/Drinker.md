# Drinker
*Weapon (Falchion), Uncommon*

You gain a +1 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls made with this magic weapon.  

You deal an additional 1d6 Necrotic damage on a Hit. You gain the same number of HP as the amount of Necrotic damage you deal.

# Drinker
*Weapon (Falchion), Rare*

You gain a +2 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls made with this magic weapon.  

While you hold this weapon, you have Advantage on saving throws made to avoid or end the Charmed and Frightened conditions.

You deal an additional 1d6 Necrotic damage on a Hit. You gain the same number of HP as the amount of Necrotic damage you deal.

---

# Drinker
*Weapon (Falchion), Very Rare*

You gain a +3 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls made with this magic weapon.  

While you hold this weapon, you have Immunity to the Charmed and Frightened conditions.

You deal an additional 1d6 Necrotic damage on a Hit. You gain the same number of HP as the amount of Necrotic damage you deal.

---
*Drinker is a blade of fine blue steel, hilted with steel in single cross-quillon and a plain, spherical, polished knob pommel. Set in the heart of the tang, where the quillons meet just above the fine chainwrapping of the hilt, is a large, one-inch diameter kite-cut black sapphire.*
