# Nightwatcher
*Weapon (Falchion), Uncommon*

You gain a +1 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls made with this magic weapon.

You gain Darkvision with a range of 60 feet. If you already have Darkvision, its range increases by 60 feet.

When planted firmly into the ground, Nightwatcher hums loudly whenever an enemy comes within 120 feet of it.

# Nightwatcher
*Weapon (Falchion), Rare*

You gain a +2 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls made with this magic weapon.

You gain Darkvision with a range of 60 feet. If you already have Darkvision, its range increases by 60 feet.

Any area of Darkness created by a spell that overlaps a 30-foot Emanation originating from the sword is dispelled.

When planted firmly into the ground, Nightwatcher hums loudly whenever an enemy comes within 120 feet of it.

# Nightwatcher
*Weapon (Falchion), Very Rare*

You gain a +3 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls made with this magic weapon.

You gain Darkvision with a range of 120 feet. If you already have Darkvision, its range increases by 120 feet.

Any area of Darkness created by a spell that overlaps a 30-foot Emanation originating from the sword is dispelled.

When planted firmly into the ground, Nightwatcher hums loudly whenever an enemy comes within 120 feet of it.

*Dragon Magazine 188*

*Nightwatcher has a very sharp, black-adamantite blade and a silver-and-ivory pommel set with a large black pearl. The blade's bearer can see at night under an open sky as well as if it were daylight. When planted firmly into the ground, Nightwatcher hums loudly if anyone with intent to harm its wielder approaches.*
