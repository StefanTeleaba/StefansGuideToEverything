*Homebrew*
# Corrosive Flail
*Weapon (Flail), Rare*

You gain a +1 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls made with this magic weapon and the target takes an additional 1d6 Acid damage. 

***Graze.*** If your attack roll with this weapon misses a creature, you can deal damage to that creature equal to the ability modifier you used to make the attack roll. This damage is the same type dealt by the weapon, and the damage can be increased only by increasing the ability modifier.

---

*Baldur's Gate 3*

*Acid was long ago abandoned by assassins, who found poison much more reliable in spiking their target's drinks. The acid kept melting the chalices.*
