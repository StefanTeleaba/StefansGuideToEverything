# Shatterspike
*Weapon (Longsword), Uncommon*

You gain a +1 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls made with this magic weapon.

Whenever this weapon or piece of ammunition hits an object, the hit is a Critical Hit and you maximize your weapon damage dice against the target.

When you score a Critical Hit with this weapon against a Construct creature, the weapon deals one additional die of damage.

The item can't be broken.
