# One Thousand Broken Dreams
*Weapon (Longsword), Very Rare*

You gain a +3 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls made with this magic weapon.  
***Mithral.*** This weapon weighs half as much as a normal Longsword and has the Finesse and Light properties.  
***Holy.*** This weapon deals an additional 1d8 Radiant damage on a Hit.  
***Drow Bane.*** You gain a +2 bonus to attack rolls against Drow. This weapon deals an additional die of damage on a Hit against Drow.

A creature of Evil alignment who touches this sword takes 3d6 Radiant damage at the start of each of its turns.
