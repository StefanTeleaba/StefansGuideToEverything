# Phalar Aluve
*Weapon (Longsword), Rare*

*The Drow inscription on this blade appears to have been recently carved. It translates to: 'Though I have to leave you, I will dance forever in Eilistraee's Light.'*

You gain a +1 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls made with this magic weapon.

While this sword is on your person, you gain a +1 bonus to Charisma (Performance) checks.

***Melody.*** You can take a Bonus Action to cause the item to Sing or Shriek for up to 1 minute. While you hold the drawn sword, it creates a 30-foot Emanation originating from you.  
**Sing.** Whenever you and all creatures Friendly to you in the Emanation make an attack roll or a saving throw, you add 1d4 to the attack roll or save.  
**Shriek.** The sword Shrieks. Whenever creatures Holstile to you in the Emanation make an attack roll or a saving throw, they subtract 1d4 to the attack roll or save. In addition, they take 1d4 Thunder damage whenever they take damage.  
Once this property is used, it can't be used again until the next dawn.

***Finesse.*** When making an attack with a Finesse weapon, use your choice of your Strength or Dexterity modifier for the attack and damage rolls. You must use the same modifier for both rolls.
