# Reedemer
*Weapon (Longsword), Legendary*

*Also known as Wyrmsbane, the lesser mate of Wyrmslayer, the sword of Kith-Kanan.*

You gain a +1 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls made with this magic weapon.  

---
*Dragon Magazine 243, p.50*

Also known as Wyrmsbane, the lesser mate of Wyrmslayer, the sword of Kith-Kanan. Redeemer is a long sword with a +11 damage rating. It inflicts double damage (+22 rating) to dragons/draconians and triple damage (+33 rating) to black and sea dragons. Wyrmsbane is immune to the imprisoning power of Baaz draconians and allows its wielder to locate a desired object out to artillery range up to three times per day with a successful average Reason action.
