# Acid-Etched Hand Crossbow
*Weapon (Hand Crossbow), Uncommon*

You gain a +1 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls made with this magic weapon.  

Whenever you score a Critical Hit with this weapon, each creature of your choice within 5 feet of the target takes 1d6 Acid damage.

# Acid-Etched Hand Crossbow
*Weapon (Hand Crossbow), Rare*

You gain a +2 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls made with this magic weapon.  

This weapon deals an additional 1d6 Acid damage on a Hit.

Whenever you score a Critical Hit with this weapon, each creature of your choice within 5 feet of the target takes 2d6 Acid damage.

# Acid-Etched Hand Crossbow
*Weapon (Hand Crossbow), Very Rare*

You gain a +3 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls made with this magic weapon.  

This weapon deals an additional 1d6 Acid damage on a Hit.

Whenever you score a Critical Hit with this weapon, each creature of your choice within 10 feet of the target takes 2d6 Acid damage.

As an Action, you can unleash a spray of acid from the weapon. Each creature in a 30-foot Cone originating from you makes a Dexterity saving throw (DC 19), taking 8d6 Acid damage on a failed save or half as much damage on a successful one. Once this action is used, it can't be used again until the next dawn.
