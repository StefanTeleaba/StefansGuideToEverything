# Gruumsh's Other Eye
*Weapon (Sling), Rare*

*The sling is an ancient weapon; a simple construction that is little more than a small pouch attached to a leather or fabric strip, and combined with suitable ammunition.*

You gain a +2 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. On a Critical Hit, the target takes an additional 4d4 Bludgeoning damage.
