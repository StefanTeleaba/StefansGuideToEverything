# Mountain Fist
*Weapon (Greatclub), Uncommon*

*This oversized yet uncannily lightweight club was created using a clutched fist of a petrified ogre.*

You gain a +1 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. This bonus increases to +3 against Constructs and the weapon deals an additional 1d8 damage.

Your Strength score is 19 while you hold this weapon. It has no effect on you if your Strength is 19 or higher without it.
