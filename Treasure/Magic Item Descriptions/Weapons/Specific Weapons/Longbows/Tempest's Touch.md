# Tempest's Touch
*Weapon (Longbow), Rare*

You gain a +2 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls made with this magic weapon.

You have Resistance to Lightning damage while holding this weapon.

If you do not nock an arrow, a magical arrow made of electricity appears nocked and ready to fire. An arrow produced by this weapon deals Lightning damage instead of Piercing damage on a hit, and it disappears after it hits or misses its target.
