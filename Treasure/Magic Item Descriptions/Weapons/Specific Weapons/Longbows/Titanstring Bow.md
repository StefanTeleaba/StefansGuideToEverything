# Titanstring Bow
*Weapon (Longbow), Uncommon*

*The commonly held belief that archers are rangey, thin-wristed people is completely false. It takes years of training to pull a bowstring all the way back to your cheekbone, and wrists like iron.*

You gain a +1 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls made with this magic weapon and you can add your Strength modifier to the damage rolls.
