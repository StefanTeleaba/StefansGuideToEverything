# Black Mithral Dagger of Subtlety
*Weapon (Dagger), Very Rare*

You gain a +3 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls made with this magic weapon.
***Black Mithral.*** The magical properties of this weapon funciton normally inside auras of antimagic.






---
*Dragon Magazine 278, p.95*

**+3 black mithral dagger of subtlety**  
This dagger possesses a +3 bonus to attack and damage rolls in normal combat, but when used in a sneak attack, its bonus increases to +6. Its construction (black mithral) provides it with the curious property of retaining Its attack and damage bonus in antimagic or dead magic areas.
