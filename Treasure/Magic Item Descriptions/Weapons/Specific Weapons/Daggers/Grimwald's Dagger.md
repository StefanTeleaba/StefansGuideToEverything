# Grimwald's Dagger
*Weapon (Dagger), Rare*

*Grimwald's Dagger emits a continuous snarling noise, like a small and angry dog, when drawn and grasped. The snarls rise into barks whenever the blade actually strikes a living target. The dagger is named for its inventor, the wizard Grimwald, who delighted in teasing his cats.*

You gain a +1 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls made with this magic weapon.  

***Mock.*** Whenever you make an attack roll with this weapon, you can forgo dealing damage. If the attack hits, the target must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw or have the Prone and Incapacitated conditions until the start of your next turn. During that time, it laughs uncontrollably if it's capable of laughter, and it can't end the Prone condition on itself.

***Savage.*** When you make your first attack roll on your turn, you can cause the dagger to *savage* your opponent. Doing so causes the blade to emit a shrill, ululating screaming noise, audible up to half a mile away. Until the start of your next turn, the dagger functions as a +2 weapon, it deals an additional 1d4 Piercing damage and you can take one additional Attack action during this turn. Once this property is used, it can't be used again until the next dawn.

*Dragon Magazine 169, p.90*

**Grimwald's dagger**

First popularized by High Lady Alustriel of Silverymoon, these amusing and effective daggers +1 emit a continuous snarling noise, like a small and angry dog, when drawn and grasped. The snarls rise into barks whenever the blade actually strikes a living target.

Opponents touched but not wounded by the blade of a Grimwald's dagger must save vs. spells or shudder helplessly with laughter on the following round (-2 on saving throws, strength checks, armor class, and attack rolls). This effect lasts for one round but may recur each time contact is made with the blade.

Once per day, the wielder of this type of dagger can cause it to savage an opponent. This causes the blade to emit a shrill, ululating screaming noise (audible up to half a mile away in good conditions) and grants the wielder two rounds of hasted attacking (double attacks, -2 initiative bonus, and no aging). During this time, the dagger functions as a +2 weapon and its base damage is doubled.

This sort of dagger is named for its inventor, the wizard Grimwald, who delighted in teasing his cats.

**XP Value:** 2,500
