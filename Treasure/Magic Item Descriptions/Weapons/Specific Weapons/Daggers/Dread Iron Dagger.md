# Dread Iron Dagger
*Weapon (Dagger), Uncommon*

*A web of veins runs the length of the blade - it has become a slender iron mortuary for all the innocents it has tasted.*

You gain a +1 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls made with this magic weapon.

If you make an attack roll while hidden and the roll hits, you deal an additional 1d6 damage.
