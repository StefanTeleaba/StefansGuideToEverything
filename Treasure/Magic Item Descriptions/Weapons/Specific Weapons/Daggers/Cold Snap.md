# Cold Snap
*Weapon (Dagger), Rare*

*In the steel, you can see small ice formations like bubbles in a bath, and even hear them pop with crickling crackles of frosted delight.*

You gain a +1 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls made with this magic weapon.  
***Frost.*** This weapon deals an additional 1d8 Cold damage on a Hit.  
***Chillbite.*** On a hit, the target's Speed is reduced by 10 feet until the start of your next turn.



**Source:** *Baldur's Gate 3*
