# Dragon Blade
*Weapon (Greatsword), Very Rare*

*A silver dragon with sapphire eyes curls around the hilt of this powerful two-handed sword.*

You gain a +3 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. When you score a Critical Hit with it against a creature that is shape-shifted, the weapon deals one additional die of damage.

This weapon deals an additional 2d6 Cold damage on a Hit and the target's Speed is reduced by 10 feet until the start of your next turn.

While Bloodied, your Critical Range with this weapon increases by 2.

# Dragon Blade
*Weapon (Greatsword), Very Rare*

You gain a +3 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls made with this magic weapon.  
***Berserker.*** While you wield this weapon during your Rage, it deals an additional 1d8 damage on a Hit.  
***Frost.*** This weapon deals an additional 2d6 Cold damage on a Hit.  
***Slow Burst.*** When you score a Critical Hit, the target must succeed on a DC 15 Wisdom saving throw or be affected by the Slow spell for 3 rounds.
