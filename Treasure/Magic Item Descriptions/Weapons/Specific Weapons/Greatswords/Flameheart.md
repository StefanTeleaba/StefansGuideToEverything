# Flameheart
*Weapon (Greatsword), Rare*

You gain a +1 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. The bonus increases to +3 when you use the weapon to attack a Giant. When you hit a Giant with this magic weapon, that creature takes an extra 2d6 damage.

This weapon deals an additional 1d6 Fire damage on a Hit. On a Critical Hit, the target and each creature of your choice within 5 feet of it takes 4d6 Fire damage.

---

*Powers & Pantheons p.27*

*Flameheart is the favored weapon of Gwaeron Windstrom.*
