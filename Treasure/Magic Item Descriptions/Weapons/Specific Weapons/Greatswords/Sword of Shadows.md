# Sword of Shadows
*Weapon (Greatsword), Legendary*

You gain a +5 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls made with this magic weapon.

The Critical Range of this weapon increases by 1.

This weapon deals an extra 2d6 Cold damage on a Hit.

On a Critical Hit, the target and each creature of your choice within 5 feet of it takes 4d6 Cold damage.

---
*Faiths and Pantheons*

The Sword of Shadows, favored weapon of the Faerûnian traveler god Shaundakul, was a +5 anarchic icy burst keen greatsword.
