# Holy

**Price:** +1 Bonus  
**Property:** Weapon  
**Aura:** Moderate Evocation  
**Associated Spell:** -  

***Holy.*** This weapon deals an additional 1d8 Radiant damage on a Hit.  

***Holy.*** A Fiend or an Undead that touches the talisman takes 8d6 Radiant damage and takes the damage again each time it ends its turn holding or carrying the talisman.

***Holy.*** A creature of Evil Alignment that touches the talisman takes 8d6 Radiant damage and takes the damage again each time it ends its turn holding or carrying the talisman.

# Holy
**XP Value:** 5,000  
**GP Value:** 25,000  
*Greyhawk*  
As a holy sword +5, this weapon will display its true worth only in the hands of a paladin. Wielded by a paladin, it negates all spells (including wands and staves) in a radius of 10 feet, thus making the paladin virtually magic-proof.
