# Dessicating

**Price:** +1 Bonus  
**Property:** Weapon  
**Aura:** Moderate Necromancy  
**Associated Spell:** Abi-Dalzim's Horrid Wilting

***Dessicating.*** This weapon deals an additional 1d4 damage on a Hit or 1d8 damage on a Hit against Plants.
