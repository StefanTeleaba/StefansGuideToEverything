# Embers of Arask
*Wondrous Item, Uncommon*

*The coals are contained within a small treasure chest made of blood red wood with handles formed from thick, bound rope fastened to the chest by bronze melted or welded there. Smoke pours out of the cracks in the chest. It feels pleasantly heavy, but not as heavy as if it were full. Inside are dim coals and embers that smoke and crackle, formed from ancient coal found deep beneath the earth. The coals are held in a covered punctured tin container suspended by a series of metal arms holding it away from the wood of the chest.*

A command word causes the embers to ignite and become flaming. They cannot be extinguished. They deal 1d6 fire damage to anything they come in contact with.
