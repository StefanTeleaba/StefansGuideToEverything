# The Enlightened Elixir of Breaking
*Wondrous Item, Uncommon*

*0.5 lb.*

*This adamantine flask produces a dark grey liquid that tastes like metal shavings.*

After you drink this potion, whenever you hit an object with an Unarmed Strike, the hit is a Critical Hit. Additionally, when you score a Critical Hit with an Unarmed Strike against a Construct creature, you deal one additional die of damage. This effect lasts 1 hour.  
Once the flask has produced one dose of the liquid, it can't produce it again until the next dawn.

***Sentience.*** The Flask is a sentient item of Chaotic Neutral alignment, with an Intelligence of 10, a Wisdom of 18, and a Charisma of 12. It has hearing and Darkvision out to 120 feet. The item communicates telepathically with its wielder and speaks Common.

***Personality.*** The Flask encourages mortals to drink and has a habit of humming sea chanteys.
