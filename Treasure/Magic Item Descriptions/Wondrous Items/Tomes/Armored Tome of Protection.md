# Armored Tome of Protection
*Wondrous Item, Spellcasting Focus, Rare (Requires Attunement by a Bard, Sorcerer, Warlock, or Wizard)*

*This 100-page tome is bound in battle-worn plates of armor.*

You can use this book as a spellbook. Along with anything written on its pages, it can't be damaged by fire or water. In addition, the spellbook doesn't deteriorate with age.

Most of the book is blank, but the Mage Armor spell is recorded on the first page of the tome.

While you are holding this book, you can use it as a spellcasting focus for your spells, you gain a +1 bonus to your Armor Class, and you can cast Mage Armor on yourself without expending a spell slot.
