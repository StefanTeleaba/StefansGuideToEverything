# Kibbenimkedaz, the Book of the Names of Stones
*Wondrous Item, Legendary (Requires Attunement)*

*The tome is written in Druidic and has been painstakingly hand-copied. Due to its connection to the stones, the revelations obtained using this book come from the memories of the rocks themselves.*

You can use the Kibbenimkedaz to cast one of the following spells: Legend Lore. Once the Kibbenimkedaz has been used to cast a spell, it can't be used to cast that spell again until the next dawn. The spells use your spellcasting ability and spell save DC.
