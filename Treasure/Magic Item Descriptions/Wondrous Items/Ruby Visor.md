# Ruby Visor
*Wondrous Item, Rare (Requires Attunement)*

While wearing this visor, you gain the following benefits.

***Force Bolt.*** As a Magic action, you can hurl three beams of force (+9 to hit). You can direct the beams at the same target or at different ones. Make a separate attack roll for each beam. Make a ranged spell attack against one creature or object in range. On a hit, the target takes 1d10 Force damage.

***Force Blast.*** As a Magic action, you can launch a blast of force (+9 to hit). Make a ranged spell attack against one creature or object in range. On a hit, the target takes 3d10 Force damage.

***Force Beam.*** As a Magic action, you can unleash a beam of force in a 5-foot-wide, 60-foot-long Line. Each creature in the Line makes a Dexterity saving throw. On a failed save, a creature takes 10d10 Force damage. On a successful save, it takes half as much damage only. Once this property is used, it can't be used again until the next dawn.
