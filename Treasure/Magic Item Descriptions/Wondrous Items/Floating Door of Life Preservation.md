# Floating Door of Life Preservation
*Wondrous Item, Legendary*

This unremarkable looking large door seems utterly useless upon first glance. More likely to be seen attached to a door frame on a fancy cruise liner, once it is in the water is where the magic really happens.

This 8ft by 5ft door is obviously large enough to fit multiple creatures on it when floating on the water’s surface, but oddly, once a single creature is upon it, no others seem to be able to. Even more odd is that if a creature named ‘Jack’ attempts to get on, they immediately slip back off.

While floating on this bizarre item, a creature is immune to all damage, cannot be removed from it without them wishing to, and has no need for food or drinking water. Additionally, they heal 1hp per minute for up to 24 hours per use (they must remain on the door this entire time).
