# Stone of Nightsong
*Wondrous Item, Legendary (Requires Attunement)*

*The stone is black and perfectly spherical, surrounded by a set of nightingale's wings.*

You can use the Stone of Nightsong to cast one of the following spells: Spare the Dying, Ceremony, Gentle Repose, and Speak With Dead. Once the Stone of Nightsong has been used to cast a spell, it can't be used to cast that spell again until the next dawn. The spells use your spellcasting ability and spell save DC.
