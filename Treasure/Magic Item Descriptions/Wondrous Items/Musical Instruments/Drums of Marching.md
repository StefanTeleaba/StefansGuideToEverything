# Drums of Marching
*Wondrous Item, Rare*

While you are playing this musical instrument, you can give an inspiring performance, bolstering your marching allies (including yourself).

At the end of each additional hour of travel beyond 8 hours, you can attempt a Charisma (Performance) check. The DC is 10 plus 1 for each hour past 8 hours. On a successful check, creatures within a 30-foot Emanation originating from you who can see or hear you, gain a bonus equal to your Charisma modifier to the Constitution saving throws required for Extended Travel. If you are a Bard, the size of the Emanation increases to 60 feet and the bonus is equal to your Performance check modifier.

*Magic Item Compendium v3.5, p.158*

---
*Dungeon Master's Guide v5.5, p.38*  
***Extended Travel.*** Characters can push themselves to travel for more than 8 hours per day, at the risk of tiring. At the end of each additional hour of travel beyond 8 hours, each character must succeed on a Constitution saving throw or gain 1 Exhaustion level. The DC is 10 plus 1 for each hour past 8 hours.
