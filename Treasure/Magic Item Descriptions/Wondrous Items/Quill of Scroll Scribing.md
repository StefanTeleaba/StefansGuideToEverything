# Quill of Scroll Scribing
*Wondrous Item, Rare*

This quill stores spells cast into it and transfers them on scrolls, creating Spell Scrolls. The quill can store only one spell at a time.

Any creature can cast a spell into the quill by touching the quill as the spell is cast. The spell has no effect other than to be stored in the quill. If the quill already held a spell, it is replaced by the new spell and the scribing process must start from the begining.

The quill functions up to 8 hours per day and picks up where it left off the next day. You must provide rare inks and scrolls according to the cost specified in the Spell Scroll Cost table (*Player's Handbook v5.5, p.233*).
