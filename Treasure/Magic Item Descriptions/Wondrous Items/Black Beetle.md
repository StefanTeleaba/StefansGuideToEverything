# Black Beetle
*Wondrous Item, Rare*

*This object is a black metal disk in the form of a beetle, with red eyes and golden markings on its back.*

This item has four command words, each requiring a Magic action to use:  
***First Command Word.*** The beetle unfolds into a Rowboat.  
***Second Command Word.*** The beetle unfolds into a Keelboat.  
***Third Command Word.*** The Boat folds back into a beetle if no creatures are aboard. Any objects in the vessel remain outside the beetle as it folds.  
***Fourth Command Word.*** The beetle unfolds into a protective shell that covers your body, giving you Immunity to Fire damage for 10 minutes or until you doff the beetle as a Magic action. Once this property is used, it can't be used again until the next dawn.

When the beetle becomes a vessel, its weight becomes that of a normal vessel its size and it has Immunity to Fire damage.

Statistics for the Rowboat and Keelboat appear in the Player's Handbook. If either vessel is reduced to 0 Hit Points, the Folding Boat is destroyed.
