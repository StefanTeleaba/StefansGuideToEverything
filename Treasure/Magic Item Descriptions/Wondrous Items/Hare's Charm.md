# Hare's Charm
*Wondrous Item, Uncommon (Requires Attunement)*

*The charm hangs from a leather cord. It is formed from beautiful polished "smoother than moonlight" dappled gray jasper carved to depict a hare curled up in a resting, teardrop-shaped position.*

While wearing this amulet, you can cast Jump from it, but can target only yourself when you do so.
