# Ship in a Bottle
*Wondrous Item (Vehicle), Varies*

This glass bottle contains a magically compressed vessel. As an Action, you can smash the bottle on a solid surface. The bottle shatters, and a fully formed ship appears in the nearest unoccupied space on water within 30ft that can fit the size of the ship. If there isn't a significant body of water to summon the ship, nothing happens and the bottle is wasted. The summoned ship is real, nonmagical, and persists indefinitely.

***Common Vessels.*** Rowboat, Canoe  
***Uncommon Vessels.*** Whaleboat, Light Fishing Vessel  
***Rare Vessels.*** Keelboat, Sailing Ship  
***Very Rare Vessels.*** Galley, Warship
