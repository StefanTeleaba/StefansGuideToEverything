# Skin of the Spider
*Wondrous Item, Rare*

*This midnight-blue skin is highlighted with a red spiderweb pattern and large black-rimmed white eyes.*

You can don or doff this item as a Magic action.

***Hiding on Ceilings.*** If you are on a ceiling and Lightly Obscured, you can take the Hide action as long as any enemy that could see you is below you.

***Spider Sense.*** When you make a saving throw and take damage, you can take a Reaction to halve the damage you take (round down).

---
This magic armor appears as a pot of bubbling black ichor. When you attune to it, the ichor adheres to and contours to your skin, and the pot disappears. The armor can be worn under normal clothes, and it doesn't impede bodily functions. Once you put it on, it can't be removed unless you choose to do so or you die, at which point the pot reappears and the ichor flows back into it.  
The armor also doesn't impose disadvantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks.

**Source:** *Magic Item Compendium v3.5, p.172*
