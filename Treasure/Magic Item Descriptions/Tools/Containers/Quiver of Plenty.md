# Quiver of Plenty
*Wondrous Item, Uncommon*

This quiver produces its own ammunition, automatically creating one *Arrow* when the wielder would draw one from the quiver. The ammunition created by the weapon vanishes the instant after it hits or misses a target.

# Quiver of Plenty
*Wondrous Item, Rare*

This quiver produces its own ammunition, automatically creating one *+1 Arrow* when the wielder would draw one from the quiver. The ammunition created by the weapon vanishes the instant after it hits or misses a target.
