# Crossbow Bolt Case of Plenty
*Wondrous item, Uncommon*  

This case produces its own ammunition, automatically creating one *Bolt* when the wielder would draw one from the case. The ammunition created by the weapon vanishes the instant after it hits or misses a target.

# Crossbow Bolt Case of Plenty
*Wondrous item, Rare*  

This case produces its own ammunition, automatically creating one *+1 Bolt* when the wielder would draw one from the case. The ammunition created by the weapon vanishes the instant after it hits or misses a target.
