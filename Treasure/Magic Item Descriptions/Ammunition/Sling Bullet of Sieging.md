# Sling Bullet of Sieging
*Wondrous Item, Rare*

*This round polished stone pebble seems to vibrate with anticipation.*

When you take the Attack action, you can replace one of your attacks with throwing a Sling Bullet of Sieging. Target one creature or object you can see within 20 feet of yourself. The target must succeed on a Dexterity saving throw (DC 8 plus your Dexterity modifier and Proficiency Bonus) or take 3d10 Bludgeoning damage as the pebble expands to the size of a boulder.

Alternatively, this bullet can be used with a Sling. You make a ranged attack with the Sling. On a hit, the target takes Bludgeoning damage equal to 3d10 plus your Dexterity modifier.

*Magic Item Compendium v3.5, p.150*

# Sling Bullet of Sieging
*Wondrous Item, Very Rare*

*This round polished stone pebble seems to vibrate with anticipation.*
