# Mithral Serpent Plate
*Armor (Half Plate Armor), Rare*

You gain a +1 bonus to Armor Class while you wear this armor and you can apply your full Dexterity modifier when determining your Armor Class. This armor does not impose Disadvantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks.

*Armor (Half Plate Armor), Very Rare*
