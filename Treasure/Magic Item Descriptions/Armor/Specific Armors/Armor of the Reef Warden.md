# Armor of the Reef Warden
*Armor (Studded Leather Armor), Rare*

*This shark skin armor is decorated with fish and shell motifs and studded with polished red coral studs.*

You gain a +1 bonus to Armor Class while you wear this armor.

While wearing this armor, you can breathe underwater, and you have a Swim Speed equal to your Speed. In addition, if you start your turn underwater with 0 Hit Points, you immediately regain 1d4 Hit Points. The armor can't heal anyone again until the next dawn.

***Blood Frenzy.*** You have Advantage on attack rolls against Bloodied creatures.
