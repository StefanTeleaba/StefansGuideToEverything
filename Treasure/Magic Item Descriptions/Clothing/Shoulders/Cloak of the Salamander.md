# Cloak of the Inferno Master
*Wondrous Item, Very Rare*

You have Immunity to Fire damage while you wear this cloak.

***Fire Aura.*** At the end of each of your turns, each creature of your choice in a 5-foot Emanation originating from you takes 2d6 Fire damage.

***Flame Spear.*** You can evoke a fiery spear in your free hand as a Bonus Action. A spear produced in this way deals Fire damage instead of Piercing damage on a hit, and if you throw it, it disappears after it hits or misses its target. Until it disappears, the spear emits Bright Light in a 10-foot radius and Dim Light for an additional 10 feet.

*Homebrew*
