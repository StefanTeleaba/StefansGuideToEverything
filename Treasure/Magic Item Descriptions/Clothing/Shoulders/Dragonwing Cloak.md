# Dragonwing Cloak
*Wondrous Item, Rare*

*This cloak has been fashioned from the wings of a red dragon wyrmling that have been lined with silk and hemmed with a fine flame-like pattern of copper and gold threads.*

While wearing this cloak, you can grip the edges of the cloak and use it to gain a Fly Speed of 40 feet. If you ever fail to grip the cloak's edges while flying in this way, you lose this Fly Speed.

***Interpose Cloak.*** If you're subjected to an effect that allows you to make a Dexterity saving throw to take only half Fire damage, you can take a Reaction to take no damage if you succeed on the saving throw and only half damage if you fail.

*Homebrew*
