# Cloak of the Crow
*Wondrous Item, Uncommon*

*The cloak of the crow was a high-collared black cape.*

While wearing the cloak, you can cast Polymorph on yourself, shape-shifting into a Giant Raven. While in that form, you retain your Intelligence, Wisdom, and Charisma scores. The cloak can't be used this way again until the next dawn.

*Homebrew*
