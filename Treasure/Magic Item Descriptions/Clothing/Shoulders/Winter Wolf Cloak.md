# Winter Wolf Cloak
*Wondrous Item, Rare*

If you take Cold damage while wearing this cloak, the cloak reduces the damage you take by 10.

In addition, while wearing this cloak, you and everything you wear and carry are unharmed by temperatures of 0 degrees Fahrenheit or lower.

***Icy Blast.*** As an Action, you can twirl the cloak to unleash a blast of cold air. Each creature of your choice in a 15-foot Emanation originating from you makes a Constitution saving throw, taking 4d8 Cold damage on a failed save or half as much damage on a successful one.

*Homebrew*
