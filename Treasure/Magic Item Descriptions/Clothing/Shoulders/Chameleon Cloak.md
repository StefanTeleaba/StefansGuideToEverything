# Chameleon Cloak
*Wondrous Item, Rare*

While you wear this cloak, Wisdom (Perception) checks made to perceive you have Disadvantage, and you have Advantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks.

If you do not move for 1 minute, you gain the Invisible condition. You become visible immediately after you move, make an attack roll, deal damage, or cast a spell.
