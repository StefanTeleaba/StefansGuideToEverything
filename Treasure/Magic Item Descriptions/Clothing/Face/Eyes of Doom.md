*Homebrew*
# Eyes of Doom
*Wondrous Item, Rare*

These crystal lenses fit over the eyes. They have 7 charges and regain 1d4+3 charges daily at dusk. The saving throw DC for all the effects of these lenses is 15.

While wearing them, you sense the condition of creatures near death that you can see within 60 feet of yourself. You sense whether a creature is *Bloodied*, if it has fewer than 5 Hit Points or if it is dead. You also know if a creature is *Undead*, if it is neither dead or alive (a *Construct*) or if it is under the effects of the *Feign Death* spell.

You can expend 1 charge to cast *Command* ("flee" or "grovel" only) or 3 charges to cast *Fear* (60-foot Cone) from them.

Alternatively, you can take a *Magic* action to expend 1 charge and fill a creature within 60 feet of you with dread. The creature must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw or have the *Frightened* condition for 1 minute. A creature that fails the save repeats it at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on itself on a success. A creature that succeeds on its save is immune to this effect for 24 hours.

*Dungeon Master's Guide v3.5, p.256*
# Eyes of Doom
These crystal lenses fit over the user’s eyes, enabling him to cast doom upon those around him (one target per round) as a gaze attack, except that the wearer must take a standard action, and those merely looking at the wearer are not affected. Those failing a DC 11 Will save are affected as by the doom spell. If the wearer has only one lens, the DC of the saving throw is reduced to 10. However, if the wearer has both lenses, he gains the additional power of a continual deathwatch effect and can use fear (Will DC 16 partial) as a normal gaze attack once per week.  
Moderate necromancy; CL 11th; Craft Wondrous Item, doom, deathwatch, fear; Price 25,000 gp.
