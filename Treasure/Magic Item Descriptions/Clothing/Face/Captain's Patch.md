# Captain's Patch
*Wondrous Item, Legendary*

The basic design and the general wear and tear of this simple leather eyepatch obscure the underlying power it holds. While you are wearing this eyepatch, you gain the following benefits.

***Captain’s Sight.*** Wearing the eyepatch doesn’t impede your binocular vision, and in fact restores sight to a blind or missing eye that it is worn over. Furthermore, you have Darkvision and Truesight with a range of 60 feet. If you already have one of those senses, the range of that sense increases by 60 feet. This Truesight allows you to see through murky waters.

***Captain’s Presence.*** Whenever you roll for a Charisma (Persuasion) or Charisma (Intimidation) check and roll a 7 or lower on the d20, you can reroll the die until you roll an 8 or higher.
