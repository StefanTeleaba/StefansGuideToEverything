# Boots of the Firestrider
*Wondrous Item, Rare*

*These scaly red dragon hide boots have a heart shaped ruby affixed to the back of each heel.*

While you wear these boots, you can take a Bonus Action to click the boots' heels together. If you do, the billowing flames of a dragon blast from your feet, granting you explosive speed. For the duration, your Speed increases by 20 feet and any creature that makes an Opportunity Attack against you has Disadvantage on the attack roll.

When you move within 5 feet of a creature or an object that isn't being worn or carried, it takes 1d6 Fire damage from your trail of heat. A creature or object can take this damage only once during a turn. If you click your heels together again, you end the effect.

When you've used the boots' property for a total of 10 minutes, the magic ceases to function for you until the next dawn.

*Homebrew*
