# Amber Amulet of Vermin
*Wondrous Item, Uncommon*

*This irregularly shaped piece of natural amber is fastened at the end of a long golden chain. Within the amber a tiny creature still seems to twitch.*

As a Magic action you summon the spirit of the creature within the amulet. The amulet casts the Summon Beast spell and summons a Giant Ant (Land) in the nearest unoccupied space. The spell is cast at level 3 (+7 attack bonus) and doesn't require Concentration, but you otherwise function as the spell's caster. Once the amulet summons a creature, the amulet can't do so again until the next dawn.
