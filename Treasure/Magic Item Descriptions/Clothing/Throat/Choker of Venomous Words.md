# Choker of Venomous Words
*Wondrous Item, Rare (Requires Attunement by a Bard, Cleric, Druid, or Paladin)*

This choker clasps around your throat with a discreet hinge and then magically slithers as it resizes to fit. The choker has 5 charges and regains all expended charges daily at dawn. While wearing it, you can use a bonus action to expend 1 or more of its charges to cast either healing word (1 charge) or mass healing word (2 charges), using your spellcasting ability modifier.

While wearing the choker, whenever you cast healing word or mass healing word, you can choose to change the nature of the spell for one or more affected targets. When you do, a target affected by the changed spell must make a Constitution saving throw, using your spell save DC. On a failed save, a target takes an amount of poison damage equal to the hit points the spell would normally restore and is poisoned until the end of your next turn. On a successful save, a target takes half as much poison damage and isn't poisoned. A creature that can’t be poisoned can still be affected in this way unless its Intelligence is 4 or lower.

*"Even the sweetest words you once revered can turn bitter and poison your soul, if you twist their meaning just a little."*
