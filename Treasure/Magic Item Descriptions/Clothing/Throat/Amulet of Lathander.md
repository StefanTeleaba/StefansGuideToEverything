# Amulet of Lathander
*Wondrous Item, Rare*

*This hammered platinum amulet was fashioned by the high-level, specialty priests of Lathander.*

*Worn by a creature of Good Alignment:*  
If you start your turn with 0 Hit Points while you wear this amulet, you immediately regain 1d6 Hit Points. The amulet can't heal anyone again until the next dawn.

*Worn by a worshipper of Lathander:*  
If you start your turn with 0 Hit Points while you wear this amulet, you immediately regain 70 Hit Points. The amulet can't heal anyone again until the next dawn.

*Worn by a creature of Evil Alignment:*  
While you wear this amulet, you take 3d6 Radiant damage at the start of each of your turns.
