# Reins of the Silver Steed
*Wondrous Item, Rare*

*This amulet is made from the reins of a legendary knight's faithful steed.*

While wearing this amulet, you gain a +5 bonus to Wisdom (Animal Handling) checks made to interact with Mounts.

***Silver Steed.*** You can cast the Phantom Steed spell as a Magic action without expending a spell slot.
