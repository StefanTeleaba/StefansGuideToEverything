# Tiger Gloves
*Wondrous Item, Uncommon*

*These high striped gloves set off by deep blacks hide a set of wicked claws.*

While wearing these gloves, you can extend or retract the claws from the gloves (no action required).

When you use your Unarmed Strike and deal damage, you can choose to attack with the claws. You deal your choice of Piercing or Slashing damage equal to 1d6 plus your Strength modifier instead of the normal damage of an Unarmed Strike.

If the target has the Prone condition, the number you need to roll on the d20 to score a Critical Hit against it decreases by 1.

When you hit a Large or smaller creature with an attack roll using the claws and have Advantage on the roll, you can force the creature to make a Strength saving throw (DC 8 plus the ability modifier used to make the attack roll and your Proficiency Bonus) or have the Prone condition.
