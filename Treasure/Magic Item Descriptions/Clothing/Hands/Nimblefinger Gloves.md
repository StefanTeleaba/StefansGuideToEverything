# Nimblefinger Gloves
*Wondrous Item, Rare*

While wearing these gloves, you gain a +2 bonus to Dexterity and a +5 bonus to Dexterity (Sleight of Hand) checks.

---

*Baldur's Gate 3*  
*These supple doeskin gloves offer both comfort and protection - alongside a perfectly attuned sense of where one's fingers are, and what pocket they're picking.*
