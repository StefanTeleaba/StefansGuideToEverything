# Arcanist's Gloves
*Wondrous Item, Uncommon*

*These long sleek silken gloves are dotted with silver stars.*

The gloves have 3 charges and regain all expended charges daily at dawn. While wearing the gloves, you can expend a charge as a Bonus Action to activate them. The next 1st-Level spell you cast until the end of your turn is cast as a 2nd-Level spell.
