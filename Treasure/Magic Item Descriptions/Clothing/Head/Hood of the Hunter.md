# Hood of the Hunter
*Wondrous Item, Very Rare (Requires Attunement)*

*I've spent my life hunting. I was born for it. My body is my tightly drawn weapon, and I am always ready to strike.*

This leather hood focuses your hunter's instincts.  
***Perception.*** While wearing this hood, you have Advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks.  
***Blindsight.*** While wearing this hood, you have Blindsight with a range of 10 feet.  
***Falcon.*** Whenever you cast Hunter's Mark, a spectral falcon appears and flies around you until the spell ends. For the duration, whenever you hit a target marked by the spell with an attack, the falcon swoops down and strikes the target for an additional 1d6 Force damage.  
***Hunter's Mark.*** The hood has 4 charges and regains all expended charges daily at dawn. While wearing the hood, you can use a bonus action to expend 1 or more of its charges to cast the Hunter's Mark spell from it. This version of the spell doesn't require concentration, but the spell ends if the target drops to 0 hit points. You can mark multiple creatures simultaneously when you cast the spell in this way by expending additional charges: the number of creatures you can mark is equal to the number of charges you expend.
