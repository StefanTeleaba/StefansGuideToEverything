# Mother's Corset

While you wear this corset, you can take a Bonus Action to sprout eight spider legs from your body or dismiss them (no action required). The legs give you a Climb Speed of 40 feet, and you have a +5 bonus to Strength (Athletics) checks made to climb. In addition, when you make a Strength (Athletics) check in order to climb, you can treat a d20 roll of 9 or lower as a 10.

When you've used the corset's property for a total of 20 minutes, the magic ceases to function for you until you finish a Long Rest.
