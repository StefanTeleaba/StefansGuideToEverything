# Bracers of Thrown Weapon Fighting
*Wondrous Item, Uncommon*

While wearing these bracers, you have proficiency with weapons that have the Thrown property, and you gain a +2 bonus to damage rolls on ranged attacks made with such weapons.

*Homebrew*
