# Claws of the Jade Panther
*Wondrous Item, Rare*

*These golden bracers are lined with jaguar fur and adorned with three jade gemstones that manifest three green shimmering blades of psychic energy that extend about one foot in front of your knuckles.*

***Claws.*** You can cause the bracers to manifest shimmering claws of psychic energy or to make them dissapear (no action required). The claws are considered simple melee weapons with the Finesse and Light Properties, the Vex mastery property, and they deal 1d8 Psychic damage on a hit. Attacks made with this weapon are treated as Unarmed Strikes and they leave no mark if they deal damage.

***Tear Space.*** You create linked teleportation portals. Choose two Medium, unoccupied spaces on the ground that you can see, one space within 5 feet and the other one within 60 feet of you. A circular portal opens in each of those spaces and remains for the duration.  
The portals are two-dimensional glowing rings filled with mist that blocks sight. They hover inches from the ground and are perpendicular to it.  
A portal is open on only one side (you choose which). Anything entering the open side of a portal exits from the open side of the other portal as if the two were adjacent to each other. The portals last until the end of your turn.
