# Catstaff
*Staff, Weapon (Quarterstaff), Very Rare*

*This ebony staff is topped with a sculpted silver knob resembling a cat's head. Two cat's eye agates are inset within the cat's eyes.*

You have a +2 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls made with this magic weapon.

A hidden compartment connects to an extradimensional space that allows the staff to hold a set of Thieves' Tools. Retrieving the Thieves' Tools from the staff requires a Utilize action.

*Attuned to:*  
***Darkvision.*** You gain Darkvision with a range of 60 feet. If you already have Darkvision, its range increases by 60 feet.  
***Feline Grace.*** While this staff is on your person, your steps make no sound, regardless of the surface you are moving across. You also have Advantage on Dexterity (Acrobatics and Stealth) checks and a Climb Speed equal to your Speed.  
***Shadow.*** While holding this weapon, you can cause it to emit Darkness in a 10-foot Emmanation originating from the staff, or dispell the darkness (no action required).
