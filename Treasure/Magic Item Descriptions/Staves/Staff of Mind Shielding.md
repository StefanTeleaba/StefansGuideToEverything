# Staff of Mind Shielding
*Staff, Rare (Requires Attunement)*

You gain a +1 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls made with this magic weapon.

*Attuned to:*

While holding the staff, you can use it as a Spellcasting Focus for your spells, you gain a +1 bonus to spell attack rolls and to the saving throw DCs of your spells.

While holding the staff, you have Resistance to Psychic damage as well as advantage on Intelligence, Wisdom, and Charisma saving throws.
