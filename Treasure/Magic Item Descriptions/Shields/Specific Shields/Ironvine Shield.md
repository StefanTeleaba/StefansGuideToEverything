# Ironvine Shield
*Shield (Shield), Uncommon*

*Each whorl and groove and worm-curled twist of this shield evokes a tangle of bog-stuck trees in rain-swollen vines.*

If a creature hits you with a melee attack roll while you're holding a weapon imbued with *Shillelagh*, the creature takes Piercing damage equal to your Wisdom modifier.
