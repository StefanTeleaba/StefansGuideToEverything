# Shield of the Mage
*Shield (Shield), Uncommon (Requires Attunement)*  
*2 lb.*

*This medium round shield was forged entirely of mithral, save for the outer rim of silver.*

You are considered trained with this Shield even if you lack training with Shields.

*Attuned to:*  
You can regain one spell slot as a Magic action while holding the Shield. You can't use this property again until the next dawn.
